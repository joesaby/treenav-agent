# Select a Virtual Camera in Cisco Webex Meetings

| 1 | Join your meeting, then select Start Video or Stop Video.                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Under Camera, select your virtual camera from the list. Virtual cameras may or may not be enabled by your system administrator. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/452001-453000/452867.jpg) |
