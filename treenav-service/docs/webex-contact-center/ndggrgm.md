# Add Problem Report and Help URLs for Your Users

Before you begin

If you don't see these options, contact your partner.

You can specify a support site URL for all of your paid and trial customers.

| 1 | From the partner view in <https://admin.webex.com>, go to Settings, and scroll to Support \> Support Setup.                                                                        |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | From the customer view in <https://admin.webex.com>, go to Settings, and scroll to Support \> Support Setup.                                                                       |
| 3 | Click Send problem reports to a custom URL, enter your URL and any additional support information.                                                                                 |
| 4 | (Optional) Click Use a custom help site and enter your help site URL.                                                                                                              |
| 5 | Click Save. Any custom URLs that you enter open when people submit feedback. For reference, the URLs that you entered appear under Troubleshooting \> Status \> Support Resources. |
