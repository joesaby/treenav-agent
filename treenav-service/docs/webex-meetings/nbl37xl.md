# Join a Meeting from the Cisco Webex Meetings Virtual Desktop App

## Join from the Join a Meeting Field

| 1 | Open your virtual desktop app. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502390.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Enter a Personal Room ID, meeting number, or meeting link in the text box below the Join a Meeting heading and click Enter to join the meeting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 3 | Enter the meeting password, if necessary, and click the Join ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502392.jpg) button.  You can only join a meeting that is in progress. Periodically select the Refresh button on the page to check if the meeting list is refreshed and the meeting has started. |

## Join from the Upcoming Meeting List

| 1 | Open the virtual desktop app and sign in.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select the Join ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502392.jpg) button. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502335.jpg) |
