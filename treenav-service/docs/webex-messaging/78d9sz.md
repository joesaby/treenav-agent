# Webex App | Show more spaces in your spaces list

After you switch, you'll see the space icons are smaller, and for any team spaces, the team name is on the same line as the space name. You can hover over any long space or team names to see the full name.

- [Desktop](#id%5F130122)
- [Web](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5Fb625f209-0928-49d8-94ce-e218806e1b70)

| Click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439461.jpg), and then select Appearance \> Show compact view You can also change this in the Settings menu. In the app header, click your [profile picture](https://help.webex.com/article/m67xht/), and then click Settings \> Messaging. Go to Space list and check the **Show compact view** check box. |
