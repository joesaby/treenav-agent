# Make another participant the host and leave your Webex Personal Room meeting

In WBS33 and later, the Leave Meeting option is located in the centralized meeting controls.

| 1 | Select Leave ![](../../../../../../../en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/419001-420000/419181.JPG) in the meeting controls, and then select Leave Meeting. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427740.jpg) |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Choose a new host, and then select Leave Meeting. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427741.jpg)                                                                                                              |
