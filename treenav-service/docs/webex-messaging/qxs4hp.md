You have to be the moderator of the team to archive it.

| 1 | Go to Teams ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/461001-462000/461982.jpg), then select the team that you want to archive. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Click Archive Team.                                                                                                                                                                  |

You have to be the moderator of the team to archive it.

| 1 | Go to Messaging and tap Teams, then select the team that you want to archive. |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Details and select Archive this Team.                                     |

You have to be the moderator of the team to archive it.

| 1 | Go to Messaging and tap Teams, then select the team that you want to archive.                                                                         |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459750.jpg) and select Archive Team. |

You have to be the moderator of the team to archive it.

| 1 | Go to Teams ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/431001-432000/432000.jpg), then select the team that you want to archive. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Click Archive Team.                                                                                                                                                                  |
