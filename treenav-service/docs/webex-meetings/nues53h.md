# Export and share poll results from Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

| 1 | Navigate to the hamburger menu ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459183.jpg) and click Download results to view the analytics. For more information, see [What are Event Analytics](https://community.sli.do/analytics-and-exports-44/what-are-event-analytics-471). |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | To download poll results, click Export \> Download export and choose the data to export. For example, select Poll results to see overall results or Poll results per user to see how each of the participants voted.                                                                                                                            |
| 3 | (Optional) Share the [Infographics](https://community.sli.do/analytics-and-exports-44/what-are-event-infographics-453) and results that you exported with your participants or leadership.                                                                                                                                                      |
