# Factory reset your Cisco Headset 730

You can reset your Cisco Headset 730 to its factory default setting. A factory reset clears all saved Bluetooth connections and resets all headset settings to their default values.

When you perform a factory reset, you'll need to re-pair the USB HD Adapter to the headset.

| Simultaneously hold the Power/Bluetooth switch up and the Mute button for 4 seconds. |
