# Webex App | Put a phone call on hold

- [Desktop](#id%5F113400)
- [Mobile](#id%5F128927)

| 1 | While on a phone call, select More ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450687.jpg) and then select Hold. After you put the call on hold, your spaces list shows that it's on hold ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439092.jpg).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

| 2 | To go back to your held call, select it from the spaces list and then select Resume ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439093.jpg). Depending on the [calling options](https://help.webex.com/en-us/xga73p/Webex-Teams-Supported-Calling-Options) you've been set up with, you may be able to resume the call on your desk phone, desktop, or mobile device. If you're connected to a Cisco headset, you can also hold and resume the call from the headset. For more information, see [Make and Answer Calls using the Cisco Headset 730](https://help.webex.com/n5qci8r/) or [Use Your Cisco Headset 500 Series](https://help.webex.com/jfhvkf/). |
