# Webex App | HubSpot bot

**_Examples of Cisco-developed bots in use_**

## Add the HubSpot bot

### End of life notice

**IMPORTANT**: This bot has reached End of Life as of **December 17th, 2024**and is no longer available. For information on creating your own bot with similar functionality, see the following [Developer Guide](https://developer.webex.com/docs/api/guides/create-your-own-hubspot-bot-integrated-with-webex). 

You must first add the HubSpot bot to a new or existing space in Webex App and then link the bot to your HubSpot account.

1. In your Search bar, enter `Hubspot.bot@webex.bot`.
2. In the HubSpot bot space, click Link account to launch _app.hubspot.com_.
3. Log in with your HubSpot credentials.
4. Select the account you want to link, then click Choose Account.

## Add HubSpot notifications

After linking your account, you can choose some quick notifications or launch the HubSpot dashboard from Webex App.

| 1 | Click Manage Notifications or type help and send the message.                                             |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Choose the events you want to receive notifications for. To customize all notifications, click Dashboard. |
| 3 | Click Save to add selected events.                                                                        |

## Manage notifications

You can create custom notifications or edit existing ones in the Dashboard.

| 1 | Click Manage Notifications \> Dashboard. The bot responds with a secure link to your dashboard. |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select Click here to launch Hubspot Bot Management.                                             |
