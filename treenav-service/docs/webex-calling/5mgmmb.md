# Webex App | Make calls with your desk phone

| 1 | Go to ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/417001-418000/417356.jpg) and make sure that your desk phone's selected. If you see My Computer instead of your desk phone, select it and switch to your desk phone. If you'd prefer to use your phone to make calls, you can [connect](https://help.webex.com/article/dhgim7) to it and use it as your default calling device. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/451001-452000/451543.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Dial a number from your app or [make a Webex App call](https://help.webex.com/n84b09o/), and your call goes through your phone. Pick up your handset, use your headset, or talk over your speakerphone on your phone. Then, if you're set up with a [phone service](https://help.webex.com/nbtgrzx/), you can control your phone from the app. You can [hold](https://help.webex.com/article/npvltok), [merge](https://help.webex.com/article/v4h0h4), or [transfer](https://help.webex.com/article/5er4f3) calls. You can see everything taking place on the phone display. The dial pad in Webex App uses dual-tone multifrequency technology (DTMF) so you can: join meetings choose voicemail options navigate IVR (Interactive Voice Response) systems ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438136.jpg) |
