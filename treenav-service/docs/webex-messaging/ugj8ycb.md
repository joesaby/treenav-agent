# Webex App | Stop and Replay Animated GIFs

| 1 | While the GIF plays for the first time, you can click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/430001-431000/430842.jpg), to stop it immediately.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | After the GIF finishes looping the first time, just hover over it and click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/430001-431000/430841.jpg) to play it again. ![](../../../../../../en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/430001-431000/430872.JPG) When you go to a space, any unseen GIFs play automatically. But, if you've left a space while a GIF was playing, it won't restart automatically when you go back to the space. |
