# Set or reset your voicemail PIN

You can use your phone, User Hub, or the Webex App to manage all your voicemail and its settings. These settings sync between applications when saved.

Before you begin

- Keep in mind that Webex users can't set up a second voicemail account.
- Your administrator must have enabled the voicemail feature for you to access and configure various settings. If you’re not finding any voicemail settings such as reset pin, voicemail greetings or notifications, check with your administrator.
- You can configure all the call settings from User Hub by navigating to Settings \> Calling.  
The Calling option is available only for users with a Webex Calling license. If you don’t see the Calling option, it means you don’t have a Webex Calling license.  
If you want to find out which calling service you’re set up with, you can check from Webex App. For more details, see [Find out what calling service you have](https://help.webex.com/nbtgrzx/).

| 4 | Enter your New Voicemail PIN. The PIN should meet the following requirements: Must be between 6 and 30 digits. Must not contain repeated patterns. For example, 121212, 123123, or 13571357 is invalid. But if you enter 1235123, it is valid. Must not contain your phone number or extension in forward or reverse order. For example, your extension is 147869\. A PIN of 9147869 or 9968741 are invalid, since they contain the extension in forward or reverse. Must not be contained within your phone number or extension in forward or reverse order. For example, if your phone number is +13015551212, then a PIN of 5551212 is invalid since it is contained within the phone number in forward, and a PIN of 2121555 is invalid since it is contained within the phone number in reverse. Must not contain the same digits more than three times in a row. For example, 222211, 888888, or 9099991. Must not contain more than three consecutive ascending digits or descending digits. For example, 123456 or 987654. Must not be your current PIN in reverse order. For example, If your current PIN is 226897, you must not enter 798622 as a new PIN. Must not contain any of your last 10 PINs. This is a default rule set by Webex Calling, it is configurable by an administrator using Webex Calling API only. |
| 5 | Re-enter your PIN in Confirm Voicemail PIN and click Submit.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |

What to do next

Once the voicemail account is set up, you can check and listen to your voice messages from [Webex App](https://help.webex.com/article/8u5983/), [User Portal](https://help.webex.com/article/raxe9/), or [Phone](https://help.webex.com/article/nhved1q/), and [Configure various voicemail settings](https://help.webex.com/article/fdkhld/) such as voicemail transcription, voicemail greetings, voicemail notifications, and so on.
