# Get a post-meeting transcript by recording your meeting or webinar

Webex Assistant is available for Webex Meetings, Webex Webinars, and Webex App.

[Classic meetings started or joined from a space](https://help.webex.com/gxw5jz/) don't support Webex Assistant. In Webex App, you can also [download a transcript without recording the meeting](https://help.webex.com/lctx8k/).

| [Record a meeting](https://help.webex.com/n62735y/) to get a postmeeting transcript. See [Interact during Webex meetings from a mobile device](https://help.webex.com/nk72k34/) for more info on recording meetings on a mobile device. |
