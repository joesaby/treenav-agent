# Webex App | Change the color of your team

- [iPhone and iPad](#id%5F90863)
- [Android](#id%5F90857)

| 1 | Go to Messaging \> Teams, select the team you want to edit, and then tap Details.                                                                                              |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
