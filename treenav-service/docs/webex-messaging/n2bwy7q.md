# Webex App | Remove a space from a team

You can move spaces out of a team, but only if:

- The space isn't the team's [general space](https://help.webex.com/en-us/m4hfiu/).
- You are a team moderator.

If the team space is moderated, only the team space moderators can remove the space from the team.
