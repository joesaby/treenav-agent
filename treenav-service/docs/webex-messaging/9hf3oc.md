# Webex App | Flag important messages

Messages that you flag are added to the Flags list and sorted by the date you flagged them. You can have up to 200 flagged messages.

- [Desktop](#task%5FE3C0459188F8D1FFA30BD84391D30611)
- [iPhone and iPad](#task%5F072779D7161EB26D5F05BF3B0A3F06EF)
- [Android](#id%5F129385)
- [Web](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5F27b344c0-f56b-4def-964f-037d1ebc7950)

| Hover over the message and click More actions ![More Options button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458469.jpg), then click Flag message. You see a flag ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458604.jpg) beside the person's name and time of the message. You can refer to that flagged message anytime. Click Filter by ![Filter button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439461.jpg) to view your filters, then go to Flags. There are two ways to remove a flag, hover over the message again and click: Unflag message ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458604.jpg).More actions ![More Options button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458469.jpg), then click Unflag message. You can also flag a reply within a message thread. Hover over the reply and click on the flag ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/417001-418000/417355.jpg), click the flag again to remove it. |
