# Cisco Headset USB-C Accessory Kit

The Cisco Headset USB-C Accessory Kit contains the following accessories:

- USB-A to USB-C Adapter—connects the to devices with a USB-C port.
- USB-C Cable—connects your Cisco wireless headset to any USB-C port.

For ordering information see <https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/support/collaboration-endpoints/headset-700-series/series.html>.
