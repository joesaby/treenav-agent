# Make calls with your ATA 190-SC (analog) phone

Cisco has announced the end-of-life of the ATA 190-SC product on October 13, 2020.

## Make a call from your analog phone

Your analog phone works just like a Cisco IP phone.

| 1 | Pick up the handset and check for a dial tone.                           |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | (Optional) Dial the digits for an outside line and wait for a dial tone. |
| 3 | Dial the phone number.                                                   |

## Redial a number from your analog phone

You can call the most recently dialed phone number.

| 1 | Pick up the handset and check for a dial tone. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Dial \* then #.                                |
