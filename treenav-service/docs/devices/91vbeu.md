# Pair your Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 to a Bluetooth device

Your Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 can save up to eight different Bluetooth® devices and maintain simultaneous connections with up to two devices at once.

To get the most out of your headset, download and set up the Bang & Olufsen app on your mobile device.

| 1 | Press and hold the Bluetooth button on the bottom of the right ear cup for two seconds.                                        |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Enable Bluetooth on your device. You can enable and disable Bluetooth from the Settings menu on most call devices.             |
| 3 | Select your headset from the device list. By default, your headset will appear in the device list as Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980. |
