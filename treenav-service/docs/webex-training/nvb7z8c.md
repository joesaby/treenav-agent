# Schedule a Webex Training Session on Behalf of Another Host

To schedule a training session on behalf of someone else, that person must have [allowed another host to schedule training sessions on their behalf ](https://help.webex.com/article/nub7z8c).

If you schedule a training session for another host, both you and the other host can modify the training session on the Edit Session  page.

| 1 | Sign in to your Webex site.                                                                                                                                          |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | If you're in Modern View, [select Classic View ](https://help.webex.com/noh9c41/Cisco-Webex-Meetings-Modern-View-and-Classic-View).                                  |
| 3 | Select Webex Training  \> Host a Session  \> Schedule Training .                                                                                                     |
| 4 | Under Session and Access Information , in the Schedule for  drop-down list, select the host's name for whom you are scheduling the session.                          |
| 5 | Finish completing the Schedule Training Session  page.                                                                                                               |
| 6 | If you have completed specifying options on the Schedule Training Session  page or Edit Scheduled Training Session  page, select Schedule  or Update , respectively. |
