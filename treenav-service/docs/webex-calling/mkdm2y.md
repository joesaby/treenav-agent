# Change Your Administrator Password If You Can't Sign in to Cisco Webex Control Hub

| 1 | From the customer view in <https://admin.webex.com>, enter your email address, and click Sign In. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Can't access your account?, enter the text from the image, and click Submit.                |
| 3 | Follow the instructions in the email we send.                                                     |
