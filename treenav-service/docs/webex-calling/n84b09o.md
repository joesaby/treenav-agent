# Webex App | Call on Webex

For Webex Calling users: To invite someone to a call using an email address they must be searchable, for example, the contact is in your organization or directory. When attempting for the first time to call to a user outside your organization or directory contacts with an email address, you can:

- Initiate contact using Webex App Messaging so that they appear in your contacts.
- Use Webex Meetings when email invites are required.
