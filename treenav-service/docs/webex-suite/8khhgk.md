# Webex App | Sync your contacts with your mobile device contacts

| Tap your [profile picture](https://help.webex.com/m67xht/), go to Settings \>  General, and toggle on Contacts Integration. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450538.jpg) If you want to stop syncing your contacts, you have two options: In the app, go to Settings \>  General, and toggle off Contacts Integration. Remove your **Webex** account from your Android account settings. |
