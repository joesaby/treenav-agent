# Hide or show your people insights profile in meetings and webinars

People insights profiles are available on the Meetings desktop app, the Webex App, the Meetings app for Android or iOS, and Webex Webinars. Your Webex site and users must be [linked to Control Hub](https://help.webex.com/341eud/) or managed in Control Hub, with Directory Synchronization turned on.

People insights profiles in meetings and webinars are available for users joining meetings or webinars on US-based cluster sites, and are only available in English at this time. Users who join from non-US organizations can access the public people insights profiles of anyone who is in a meeting on a site that has people insights profiles turned on. This feature will be rolled out to more devices and platforms in the future.

For in-depth information about people insights profiles, see [People Insights Profiles: Creating a Human Collaboration Experience](https://www.cisco.com/c/dam/en/us/solutions/collaboration/people-insights-whitepaper.pdf).

If you only want to hide or show certain sections of your profile, such as your photo, a past position, or your social media links, see [Create or edit your people insights profile in meetings or webinars](https://help.webex.com/ng0s8vg/).

[Hide or show your people insights profile in meetings and webinars for account holders](#id%5F95722)

The following instructions explain how to hide or show your profile in meetings and webinars for account holders. You can also hide or show your profile from [https://people.webex.com](https://people.webex.com/) or during a meeting or webinars.
- From [https://people.webex.com](https://people.webex.com/), select Hide Profile or Unhide Profile, and go to Step 4 below.
- During a meeting or webinar, select View my People Insights profile  next to your name in the Participants panel and go to Step 3 below.

| 1 | Select your name in the top of the left navigation bar, select My Profile, and select View my People Insights profile. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/435001-436000/435536.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In your profile, select ![More](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/434001-435000/434290.jpg) and then select Hide or Unhide. ![Hide](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/435001-436000/435538.jpg)                                                                                                                        |
| 3 | The My People Insights Profile page opens and your profile is hidden or shown. Select Save. ![My People Insights profile](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433372.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 4 | To see the change in your profile, select ![Refresh](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/434001-435000/434289.jpg) at the top of your profile. When your profile is hidden, only your name shows. If you added a photo to your profile, it's hidden too. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/436001-437000/436477.jpg) |

[Hide or show your people insights profile in meetings or webinars for guests](#id%5F110435)

The following instructions explain how to hide or show your profile during a meeting or webinar. You can also hide or show your profile from https://people.webex.com. Go to [https://people.webex.com](https://people.webex.com/), select Don't have a Webex account, and follow the on-screen instructions.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, select View my People Insights profile ![View My People Insights profile](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433246.jpg) next to your name in the Participants panel. ![Particiapnts panel](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433249.jpg) Your profile appears. It may already include publicly available information that is found on public websites, such as conference profile pages. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select select ![More](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/434001-435000/434290.jpg) and then select Hide or Unhide. ![Hide](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/435001-436000/435538.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 3 | A email that contains a link to the edit page is sent to you. Select Got it. ![Email notification](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433289.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 4 | Check your inbox and click the link in the email. The email looks similar to the following: ![Verification email](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433290.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 5 | The My People Insights Profile page opens in a new browser window and your profile is hidden or shown. Select Save. ![My People Insights profile](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/433001-434000/433372.jpg)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 6 | To see the change in your profile, go back to your meeting or event and select ![Refresh](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/434001-435000/434289.jpg) at the top of your profile.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
