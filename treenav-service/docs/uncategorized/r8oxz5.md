# Delete a poll in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/). Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

Any poll that you delete from your Slido is permanently deleted.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Apps \> Slido. In Webex Suite Platform click … \> Slido (polling and Q&A).                                                          |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select the Slido that contains the poll.                                                                                                                               |
| 3 | Locate the poll and click ![More menu](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/451001-452000/451601.jpg) \> Delete. |
| 4 | Click Delete poll to confirm you want to delete the poll.                                                                                                              |
