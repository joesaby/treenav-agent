# Schedule a Webex Meeting with end-to-end encryption

- [Desktop](#task%5Fc1q%5Fd4j%5Fwpb)
- [Mobile](#Cisco%5FTask%5Fin%5FList%5FGUI.dita%5F0c02fb00-af2e-408d-b152-9a0e2b67efee)
- [Outlook](#task%5Farr%5Fy1p%5Fwpb)

Schedule a meeting enabled with end-to-end encryption:

| 1 | In Webex App or your Webex site, click Schedule \> Schedule a Meeting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select Advanced Settings, and from the Meeting type drop-down list, click Webex Meetings Pro-End to End Encryption\_VOIPOnly.                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 3 | If Digital Watermarking has been turned on by your administrator, the option to turn it on is shown in the Security section. Check the box next to Audio to turn on audio watermarking for this meeting. For more information, see [Webex App \| Add visual watermarks to shared content in a meeting](https://help.webex.com/saxcjg/). |
| 4 | Enter the rest of your meeting details, then click Schedule.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |

Enable end-to-end encryption by default for all scheduled and personal room meetings:

| 1 | [Sign in to your Webex site](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin).                          |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Click Settings  \> Scheduling.                                                                   |
| 3 | From the Meeting Type drop-down list, select Webex Meetings Pro-End to End Encryption\_VOIPOnly. |
| 4 | Click Save.                                                                                      |

To schedule a meeting enabled with end-to-end encryption on your mobile device, you must first set your default meeting type to Pro-End to End Encryption\_VOIPOnly.

Set your default meeting type:

| 1 | [Sign in to your Webex site](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin).                          |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Click Settings  \> Scheduling.                                                                   |
| 3 | From the Meeting Type drop-down list, select Webex Meetings Pro-End to End Encryption\_VOIPOnly. |
| 4 | Click Save.                                                                                      |

Schedule your meeting:

| 1 | From the Meetings app, swipe left to My Meetings.                                                                                      |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Schedule ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/440001-441000/440463.jpg). |
| 3 | Enter the rest of your meeting details, then tap Schedule.                                                                             |

| 1 | Open Microsoft Outlook and click Meeting \> Add Webex Meeting.                                                                                     |
