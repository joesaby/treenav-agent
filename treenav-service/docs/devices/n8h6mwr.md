# Remove a participant from a meeting on Board, Desk, and Room Series

In Webex Meetings and personal room meetings, the host and co-host can disconnect a participant from a meeting on a Board, Desk, or Room Series device. During Webex App meetings, everyone in the meeting can disconnect others. 

| 1 | In a meeting which you are hosting, access the call controls and tap the Participants  button. On a DX80, the participants button is on your device or controller's home screen during a call. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap the person you want to disconnect.                                                                                                                                                         |
| 3 | Tap  Remove from call . ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/448001-449000/448764.jpg)                                               |
| 4 | Webex will remove the selected participant and let them know.                                                                                                                                  |
