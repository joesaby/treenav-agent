# Monitor your Cisco Room Phone

Use the information in the following articles to monitor and troubleshoot your device. 

[View your device data from Control Hub](#task%5F54404E1FE1F7ECDEDCCC8E2CB2F72DAC)

You can view your device data with the Analytics tool in Control Hub, including device utilization and feature usage. Use this information to determine how often devices are in use or which features are popular with users. 

| 1 | From the customer view in <https://admin.webex.com>, go to Analytics. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Devices.                                                        |
| 3 | Select your device from the Type of device drop-down list.            |

[Display the Phone Information window](#task%5F74399765B1C60CC9ABDFD4FAFEAC06CB)

You can view your phone information from the Settings menu. Use this information to troubleshoot your device or to check the phone state. This information is different for each deployment model, but it includes:

- Device name.
- IP address.
- The Media Access Control (MAC) address and serial number.
- Current firmware and date of last update.

| 1 | Tap the top-left corner of the phone screen. |
| - | -------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Settings from the list of menu options.  |
| 3 | Tap About this device.                       |

[View status messages](#task%5F4D5E04BE532EC920AC88867CE2B22265)

After the phone has registered, you can view a list of system messages. This is a useful tool to troubleshoot issues or to view the phone network activity. 

| 1 | Tap the top-left corner of the phone screen.        |
| - | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Settings from the list of menu options.         |
| 3 | Navigate Issues and diagnostics \> Status messages. |
