# Smart audio options for Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars

| 1 | In a meeting, click Show Menu Bar \> Meeting  \> Meeting settings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In a webinar, click Show Menu Bar \> Webinar  \> Webinar settings. You can also click the drop-down arrow on the ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450676.jpg) for audio settings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 3 | For audio settings, click Audio and then scroll to Webex smart audio. Noise removal—Removes all background noise. Optimize for my voice—Removes all background noise and background speech. Optimize for all voices—Removes all background noise and enhances all voices nearby. Music mode—Others hear the original sound when you play an instrument or sing. For more information on music mode, see [Enable music mode in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars](https://help.webex.com/article/k0yf2g/Enable-Music-Mode-in-Webex-Meetings-and-Webex-Events-%28New%29). |
