# Webex App | Turn on music mode in calls and meetings

When a call or meeting begins, the microphone optimizes audio for speech by default. To optimize for music, turn on music mode.

| During a call or meeting, click the drop-down in Mute ![Mute](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450675.jpg) and then select Music mode under Webex smart audio. To set this as the default for all your calls and meetings, [turn on music mode in your audio settings](https://help.webex.com/n139bv9/). You'll see a musical note in Mute ![Mute](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/454001-455000/454434.jpg), letting you know that music mode is on and you're ready to rock. |
