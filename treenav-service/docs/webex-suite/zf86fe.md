# Adjust your computer mic and speaker volume in a Webex Meeting

- [WBS40.9 and Later](#id%5F138006)
- [WBS40.6.x and Earlier](#id%5F81327)

For 41.10 and later sites, see [Select audio settings for Webex Meetings](https://help.webex.com/article/idbbo9/Select-audio-settings-for-Webex-meetings).

To see what version you're on, see [Find your Webex Meetings version number](https://help.webex.com/article/qgij81/Find-Your-Cisco-Webex-Meetings-Version-Number).

| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Use the sensitivity sliders to adjust your speaker and microphone volume. You can select Automatically adjust volume for your microphone volume.                                                                                                                                                |
| 3 | Select Test under Speaker to hear the volume at its current setting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
