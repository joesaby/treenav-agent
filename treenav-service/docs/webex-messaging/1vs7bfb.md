# Enable Restrictions for Guests in Team Spaces

| 1 | From the customer view at <https://admin.webex.com>, go to Services and then click Settings on your Messages card. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Scroll to Team Guest Restrictions and then toggle Enable restrictions for team guests to on.                       |
