# Answer calls on your Analog Telephone Adaptor (ATA) 190-SC

Cisco has announced the end-of-life of the ATA 190-SC product on October 13, 2020.

## Answer a call on your ATA phone

Your analogue phone (ATA) works just like a Cisco IP phone.

| When your analog phone rings, pick up the handset to answer the call. |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Answer call waiting on your ATA phone

When you're on an active call, you know that a call is waiting when you hear a single beep.

| 1 | Perform a hook flash to speak with another caller who is calling you. You connect to the second caller and put the first caller on hold.                                   |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | (Optional) To speak with the first caller, perform a hook flash again. Subsequent hook flashes toggle you between the two callers. You can't create a conference this way. |
