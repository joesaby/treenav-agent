# Webex App | Turn off your video during a meeting or call

The meeting host can stop your video if you had to step away or if there’s something distracting in your background.

If you prefer, you can [turn off your video by default when you join meetings](https://help.webex.com/npg35it/).

- [Windows and Mac](#task%5FB2A33BF7CBBA8A33EF8FA623D486A3A7)
- [iPhone, iPad, Android](#task%5F7957C80A4C235C15C0AECAA03186C8A4)
- [Web](#id%5F93246)

Your video is on by default when you join a meeting or call, so you will see a gray icon .

| To turn off your video, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450680.jpg). You can tell your video is turned off when the icon turns red ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450406.jpg). |

Webex App supports [different call and video options](https://help.webex.com/article/z94f4f), depending on how your account is setup.

| In your meeting or call, tap ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450683.jpg) to turn off your video. You can tell your video is turned off, when the icon turns red ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450682.jpg). If you prefer, you can turn off your video before making or answering a call or joining a meeting. See [Join a Meeting or Call with Audio Only](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/dwpxd0) or [Answer a call](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/n19ix1l) for more details. |
