# Enable High-Quality or High-Definition Video for Your Webex Site in Cisco Webex Site Administration

## Enable High-Quality Video for Your Webex Site

High-quality video must be turned on for a Webex site in order for high-definition video to be available. 

High-definition video is an optional feature that must be provisioned for a Webex site. 

You can also turn on high-quality video in the Default Scheduler Options section.

| 1 | Sign in to Webex Site Administration and go to Configuration \> Common Site Settings \> Options.                              |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In the Site Options section, check the Turn on high-quality video (360p) (Meetings, Training, Events, and Support) check box. |
| 3 | Click Update.                                                                                                                 |

## Enable High-Definition Video for Your Webex Site

High-quality video must be turned on for a Webex site for high-definition video to be available. 

High-definition video is an optional feature that must be provisioned for a Webex site. 

This setting only applies to Webex Meetings, Webex Training, and Webex Events.

You can also turn on high-quality video and high-definition video in the Default Scheduler Options section.

| 1 | Sign in to Webex Site Administration and go to Configuration \> Common Site Settings \> Options.                            |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In the Site Options section, from the Set maximum video bandwidth to list, choose a resolution to apply for Webex Meetings. |
| 3 | Check the Turn on high-definition video (720p) (Meetings and Training) check box.                                           |
| 4 | Click Update.                                                                                                               |
