# Webex App | Peek Into a Space

- [Desktop and Web](#id%5F124208)
- [Mobile](#id%5F136739)

| Go to Messaging ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438646.jpg), right-click an unread space, and then click Peek. You'll see the peek mode icon ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439340.jpg) in the messaging area to remind you that you're peeking in a space. To stop peeking in a space, navigate away from the space, and then click on the space to read as normal. |
