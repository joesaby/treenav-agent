# Connect the Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 USB cable

## Connect the Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 USB cable

Use the included USB cable to connect your headset with your laptop or Webex Desk device.

Audio over Bluetooth® will always take priority over a wired connection. Disconnect other Bluetooth connections if you want to switch to a wired connection.

| 1 | Plug the small end of the USB cable into the port at the bottom of the right ear cup. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Connect the large end of the cable to your intended device.                           |
| 3 | Press the Power button to turn on your headset.                                       |
