# Prevent users from self-registering with your domain

We recognize that there are situations where you want more control of the user onboarding process, so we provide an option to prevent users from signing up when their email address is from one of your domains. Disabling Self-Registration prevents new users from self-registering an account in your organization.

For a discussion of the potential problems with self-registration, see [Ways to add users to your Control Hub organization](https://help.webex.com/nj34yk2).

Before you begin

This feature is applicable only when you have [claimed one or more of your domains](https://help.webex.com/cd6d84), and it applies to any user with an email address from those domains.

| 1 | Sign in to [Control Hub](https://admin.webex.com/).                                                                         |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click on Organization Settings and scroll to Self-Registration. The control is disabled if you haven’t claimed any domains. |
| 3 | Toggle self-registration Off.                                                                                               |

What to do next

Read [Ways to add users to your Control Hub organization](https://help.webex.com/nj34yk2) to find the best option for bringing users into your Webex organization. We also recommend reading [Assign organization account roles](https://help.webex.com/fs78p5/) to grant people the rights to manage users.
