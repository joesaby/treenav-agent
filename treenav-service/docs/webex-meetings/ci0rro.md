# Start a Personal Room meeting

| Sign in to [User Hub](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin), then select Start. The meeting starts in the desktop or web app, depending on what you used last. Once you've started your Personal Room meeting, you can see and hear any attendees who join. |
