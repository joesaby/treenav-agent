# Configure participant sharing options for your site

- [Control Hub ](#task%5FFA1D33E6B9E7CC1CB7A60644393982D9)
- [Site Administration](#task%5F0FDCF959C649B1057CEEA324613F6C77)

After you enable participant sharing for your Webex sites, hosts can choose whether to allow participants to share in their meetings.

| 1 | From the customer view in [https:/​/​admin.webex.com](https://admin.webex.com), go to Services and under Meetings, select Site.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Choose the Webex site that you want to change settings for, and then select Configure Site.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 3 | Under Common Settings, select Site Options.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 4 | In the Site Options section, check Allow Participants to Share in meetings (Meetings only). When you uncheck this check box, the following settings aren’t available: The first attendee to join will be the presenter (when scheduling a meeting) Anyone Can Share (in the host's Participant menu during a meeting) When you uncheck this check box, attendees can share if the host assigns them the Presenter role. |

After you enable participant sharing for your Webex sites, hosts can choose whether to allow participants to share in their meetings.

| 1 | Sign in to Webex Site Administration and go to Configuration \> Common Site Settings \> Options.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In the Site Options section, check Allow Participants to Share in meetings (Meetings only). When you uncheck this check box, the following settings aren’t available: The first attendee to join will be the presenter (when scheduling a meeting) Anyone Can Share (in the host's Participant menu during a meeting) When you uncheck this check box, attendees can share if the host assigns them the Presenter role. |
