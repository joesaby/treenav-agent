# Compare Webex Meetings Types

See the table and following descriptions for more details.

| Feature                                             | Standard Scheduled Meeting | Personal Room Meeting  |
| --------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Schedule single occurrence and recurring meetings   | ✔                          | ✔                      |
| Maximum number of participants                      | 1,000                      | 1,000                  |
| Can require attendees to register                   | ✔                          |                        |
| Allow attendees to join before the meeting starts   | ✔                          |                        |
| Add cohosts or alternate hosts                      | ✔                          | ✔                      |
| Allow other hosts to schedule meetings on my behalf | ✔                          | ✔                      |
| Link to join the meeting                            | Unique URL                 | Your Personal Room URL |
| Join by video system                                | ✔                          | ✔                      |

## Standard Scheduled Meeting

You can [schedule a standard meeting from your Webex site](https://help.webex.com/article/4a6d0z), [in Microsoft Outlook using the Webex Meetings Desktop App](https://help.webex.com/article/nboahd2), or from [your mobile device](https://help.webex.com/article/qadfrk).

- You can schedule single occurrence or recurring meetings.
- You can choose to require registration when you schedule the meeting.
- You can choose to allow attendees to join the meeting up to 15 minutes before start time when you schedule the meeting.
- You can add alternate hosts and [let anyone with a host account on the Webex site host the meeting](https://help.webex.com/article/b5z6he).
- [Someone with a host account on the Webex site can schedule a meeting on your behalf](https://help.webex.com/article/nmk7hvv). You are the only host who can start the meeting.
- Each meeting has a unique meeting URL that users click to join.
- Each meeting has a unique video address that users dial if they join by video system.

## Personal Room Meeting

[Personal Room meetings](https://help.webex.com/article/nul0wut) are best for impromptu meetings, and back-to-back meetings in which the meeting host wants to remain in one meeting room for several meetings and have attendees join at different times.

- You can [start a Personal Room meeting from your Webex site](https://help.webex.com/article/nyiuyli). You can [schedule single occurrence and recurring Personal Room meetings in Outlook using the Webex Meetings Desktop App](https://help.webex.com/article/nboahd2).
- Attendees can't join before the meeting starts but, during a meeting, you can [lock your Personal Room](https://help.webex.com/article/vjfafi). People wait in a virtual lobby until you admit them. You can also set your Personal Room to lock automatically zero to 20 minutes after the meeting starts.
- You can add cohosts or alternate hosts to [let others host your Personal Room meetings without you](https://help.webex.com/n1bqkbv/).
- Your cohosts or alternate hosts can schedule personal meetings on your behalf.
- All Personal Room meetings use your Personal Room URL as the meeting link that users click to join. Your Personal Room URL is in the format company.webex.com/meet/ username.
- All meetings use your Personal Room video address as the video address that users dial if they join by video system.
