You have access to a large library of emojis, and can choose from different categories and color tones.

| When writing a message, select emoji, choose the emoji that you want to add, and send the message. You can also search for an emoji using the search box. You can copy and paste emojis from the message area to add them to space or team names. Emojis that you choose often, appear in the Frequently usedsection. You can also [type an emoticon into your message](https://help.webex.com/en-us/article/na2v31d/), leaving a space after the text, and Webex App automatically changes it to an emoji. You can also use [emoji shortcodes](https://help.webex.com/article/0rv1uu/) to instantly add emojis to your message. |
