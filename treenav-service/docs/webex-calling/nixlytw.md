# Webex Calling Regional Media for Cloud Connected PSTN (CCP)

If your PSTN provider is connected to your local region, then regional media for Cisco Calling Plan(CCP) routes your PSTN traffic locally, resulting in improved call quality. If your PSTN provider isn’t connected to your local region, your PSTN traffic is routed to your organization’s home region.

Webex Calling Regional Media for CCP does not apply to Webex room systems.

You must first meet the network requirements as set by Cisco. To avoid a service outage, we recommend that you update your network, as necessary. For reference, see [Network Requirements for Webex Services](https://help.webex.com/en-us/WBX000028782/Network-Requirements-for-Webex-Services).

Available for: 

- Webex Calling for Service Providers (SP)
- Webex Calling for Value Added Resellers (VAR)

The following regions are combined into one [Production Port Reference](https://help.webex.com/b2exve/) table for SPs and VARs:

- U.S.
- Canada
- EMEA
- Australia
- Japan
