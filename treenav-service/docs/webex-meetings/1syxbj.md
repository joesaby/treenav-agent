# View upcoming meetings on User Hub

From the Meetings section of your Webex site, you can view a list of your next four meetings, events, and training sessions. See the title, time, and host for the meeting. Shortly before the meeting, participants see a Join button. Meeting hosts have a Startbutton.

Clicking a meeting shows you the complete meeting details, including the meeting link, more ways to join, and a button to add the meeting to your calendar.

If you're the host, you also have the host key and buttons to copy the meeting details, edit the meeting, and cancel the meeting.

| 1 | Sign in [User Hub](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin).        |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | To view all upcoming meetings, click Calendar in the navigation bar. |
