# Still unable to pair to Webex Share?

Before you begin

- You have followed the suggested tips and tricks for pairing. See [Tips and Tricks for Pairing to Cisco Webex Share](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/en-us/w4el9cb).
- You have turned Automatic Pairing on. See [Turn Automatic Pairing on From Your Windows Computer](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/en-us/7m45l8) or [Turn Automatic Pairing on From Your Mac Computer](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/en-us/6m45l8).
- You have tried to manually pair. See <https://help.webex.com/en-us/nf29igm/Manually-Connect-to-Cisco-Webex-Devices-from-Cisco-Webex>.

| 1 | In your Webex App app, click Send Feedback. Windows users: Click your profile icon and select Send Feedback.Mac users: Click Help on the menu bar and select Send Feedback. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Fill out the form and select **Webex App**Devices for the product.                                                                                                          |
