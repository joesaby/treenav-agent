# Start or Join a Meeting in the Virtual Desktop App from Your Cisco Webex Site

| 1 | Sign in to your Webex site.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | From the My Personal Room tile, select the downward arrow on the right side of the Start Meeting or Join Meeting button.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 3 | Select Always start from VDI app to open the meeting in the virtual desktop app. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502336.jpg) When you select Always choose best option for me, the first choice is to open the Webex virtual desktop app (VDI). The second choice is to open the desktop app, and the third choice is to open the web app. |
