# Webex App | Set a PIN on mobile devices

If you don't already use a PIN, then you'll get a message on your device telling you to enable one. 

You won't be able to use Webex App until you set a PIN. 

| Enable a PIN from the settings menu on your device. iPhone and iPad: Go to Settings , then choose Touch ID & Passcode . Android: Go to Settings , then choose Lock screen . |
