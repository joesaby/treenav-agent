# Optimize 3D content in meetings

Optimize for high fidelity content in Meetings. This increases the quality of 3D content such as CAD and medical imaging by making the lines on 3D models and high quality images sharper.

This feature is available by request only.

This option is not available in meetings where any single participant didn't join from a desktop or the meeting is being recorded.

You must choose Optimize for 3D from the share settings. Automatically optimize doesn't apply to 3D content.
