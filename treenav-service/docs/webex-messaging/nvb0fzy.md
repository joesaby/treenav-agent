# Webex App | Send a Voice Clip

| 1 | In the space, touch and hold the microphone ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438459.jpg), and then start talking.                                                                                                                          |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | When you're finished, let go of the microphone ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438459.jpg) to save the recording.                                                                                                                         |
| 3 | (optional) Tap Play ![play button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/443001-444000/443569.jpg) to preview your recording. If you're not happy with it, you can delete it and try again.                                                                       |
| 4 | Tap Send ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/437001-438000/437489.jpg) to send your voice clip. If you'd like to send voice clips automatically after recording, tap your profile picture and select Advanced \> Voice Clip Settings \> Send Automatically. |
