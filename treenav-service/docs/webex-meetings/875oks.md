# Set the Speaker Video Position in Cisco Webex Recordings

You can choose the position of the video for the active speaker in downloaded MP4 meeting recordings. This setting applies to recordings from Webex Meetings and Webex Events. This setting doesn’t apply to the Webex Advanced Recording Format (ARF) format.

This feature must be enabled for your site.

| 1 | Sign in to Webex Site Administration and go to Configuration \> Common Site Settings \> Options.                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In the Site Options section, select one of the following options for the speaker's video position: Inside Frame—The video appears in the top right corner and overlaps the meeting screen. Outside Frame—The video appears to the top right of the meeting screen, with no overlap. |
