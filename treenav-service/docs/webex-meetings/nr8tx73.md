# Moderate questions in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Q&A in Slido, click ⚙ and then toggle on Moderation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | When a participant asks a question, it appears under In review: Click ✓ to approve the question and make it visible to everyone.Click 𐄂 to dismiss the question. The question moves to the Archive tab.Click ⋮ to Reply to a question, add Labels or Delete the question permanently.You may need to click the question first to see these options. To stop moderating questions, click ⚙ and toggle off the Moderation option. |
