# Host controls for raise hand on Board, Desk, and Room Series

The host and co-host can lower hands from the participant list on the device to keep track of participants who still have questions. Only the host and co-host have these controls. Lowering raised hands is available in meetings where raise hand capability is available.

Note that now partcipants' hands are automatically lowered after they finish speaking, keeping the meeting clear and organized.

Lowering raised hands isn’t supported in space-backed meetings and 1:1 calls. 

Open the participant list, select the person and tap Lower hand .

To lower all hands, open the participant list, select More  and tap Lower all hands .
