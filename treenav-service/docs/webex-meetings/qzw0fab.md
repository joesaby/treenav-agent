# Hide or show non-video participants during meetings and webinars

This feature is supported on Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. On Webex App, this feature is available on our Webex Suite meeting platform. [Find out if your meetings use the Webex Suite meeting platform](https://help.webex.com/m61d8eb/).

- [Desktop](#id%5F137251)
- [Mobile](#Cisco%5FTask%5Fin%5FList%5FGUI.dita%5F8174636a-d299-4a8a-8979-fae891a1e659)
- [Web](#Cisco%5FTask%5Fin%5FList%5FGUI.dita%5Fc335cc63-9bf7-42d9-b75a-8c78b721623e)

| 1 | When you're in a meeting, click Layout ![Layout icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455202.jpg), and then toggle on Hide participants without video. On the Webex Suite meeting platform, you can also set this when you're not in a meeting. Click Settings ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427579.jpg) on the [navigation sidebar](https://help.webex.com/nx3i8h5/), select Meetings \> Layout, and check Hide participants without video. We save the setting so it applies to all future meetings.Now, you only see the participants who are showing video. An indicator on the screen shows you how many non-video participants are hidden. ![Participants with video off indicator](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/457001-458000/457856.jpg) If you [lock focus on a participant](https://help.webex.com/la12o0/) and that person stops showing their video, they remain in focus. |
