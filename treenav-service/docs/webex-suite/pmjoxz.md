# Webex App | Welcome to your home tab

You can set Home to be your default [landing screen](https://help.webex.com/en-us/article/wy517z/Webex-App-%7C-Schedule-a-Meeting-from-the-Meetings-Calendar), every time you open the app.

Home on Windows and Mac, is currently only available for free users.

- [WIndows and Mac](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5F3a193dcd-6f02-453c-8241-1ac4a0643875)
- [Mobile](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5F2d71d1fd-e72a-416e-b9a5-c039ddcb9c79)

Click Let's go in the Home screen banner and choose from the following, to quickly set up the app:

- Test your meeting setup
- Edit your profile
- Choose an app theme
- Set your notifications
- Download the mobile app

As you spend time working in the app, you'll see more content here. Now that you've set your preferences the way you like, you're on your way to working in Webex App.
