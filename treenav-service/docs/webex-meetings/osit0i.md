# Revoke Third-Party Integrations from a Webex Meetings Account

| 1 | From Modern View, go to Preferences  \> General .                                                                                                                                                   |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Under 3rd party integration , click the Revoke authorization access  link. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/431001-432000/431302.jpg) |
| 3 | Under the third-party integration that you want to revoke, click Revoke Access .                                                                                                                    |
| 4 | Click Revoke .                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 5 | Repeat steps 3-4 for every third-party integration that you want to revoke.                                                                                                                         |
