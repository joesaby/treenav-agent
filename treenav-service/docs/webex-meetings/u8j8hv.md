# Turn multimedia sharing on or off for your Webex site in Control Hub

Presenters on your site can use the Multimedia Viewer to share web-based multimedia in meetings, events, and training sessions. You can turn this feature on or off for your site.

| 1 | Sign in to [Control Hub](https://admin.webex.com/), then go to Services \> Meeting .       |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Select  Sites , choose your site, then select  Configure Site .                            |
| 3 | Under  Common Settings , select  Site Options , then check the  Allow Multimedia sharing . |
| 4 | Select Update .                                                                            |
