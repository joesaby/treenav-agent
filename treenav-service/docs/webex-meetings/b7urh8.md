# Sign in to Webex for Government

**_Webex for Government sign-in screens_**

- [Sign in to Webex for Government](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5Fd0324c16-0bb5-4191-afc9-ea8b5c379056)
- [Switch from commercial Webex App](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5Ff8b28eb6-fe00-420f-87f4-406adb003ce9)

Your administrator might provide a custom installation of Webex App preconfigured for Webex for Government.

| 1 | Start Webex App. The sign-in screen displays the FedRamp logo and Webex for Government preselected. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Sign in and provide your credentials.                                                         |

You can switch from the commercial Webex App to Webex for Government within the Webex App.

| 1 | Start Webex App. The sign-in screen displays the Webex logo and Webex selected.                                                                          |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click the down arrow beside Webex and select Webex for Government from the list. Webex App restarts to take you to the FedRAMP sign-in page.             |
| 3 | Enter your email address and check I agree to terms and conditions, then click Next to sign in.  The first time you sign in, you must set up a password. |
