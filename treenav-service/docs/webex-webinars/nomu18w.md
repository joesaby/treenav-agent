# Download files before a webinar

To learn more about turning this feature on for your Webex site, [contact your administrator](https://help.webex.com/7ksvf1/).

| 1 | Click View webinar information in your webinar invitation email. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Enter the webinar password.                                      |
| 3 | In the Webinar materials section, click the link for the files.  |
| 4 | Click OK.                                                        |
