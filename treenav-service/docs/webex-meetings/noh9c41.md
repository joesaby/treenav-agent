# Webex Meetings Modern View and Classic View

When both Modern View and Classic View are available, you can switch between the two views.

## Modern View

To switch to Classic View from Modern View, select Classic View next to your username at the top of the page.

## Classic View

To switch to Modern View from Classic View, select Modern View next to your username at the top of the page.
