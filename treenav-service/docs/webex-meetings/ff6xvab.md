# Show or hide the menu bar and meeting controls in meetings, webinars and events

## Show or hide the menu bar

 The menu bar of the meeting, webinar, or event window is hidden by default, but you can show or hide the menu at any time. 

| 1 | Click Show menu bar  to display the top menu. If you don’t see Show menu bar, check if your meetings are already running on the [Webex Suite meeting platform](https://help.webex.com/m61d8eb/). Go to [Key Changes](https://help.webex.com/m61d8eb/Webex-App-%7C-About-the-Webex-Suite-meeting-platform#reference-template%5F3739eeeb-2f7a-4984-93fd-4cf203de6dec) to find out about the enhancements available on this platform. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Hide menu bar  to hide the top menu.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |

## Show or hide the meeting controls

| 1 | Hover your mouse over the top of the meeting controls, and click Hide control bar to hide them.                      |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Hover your mouse over the bottom of the Meetings app window and click Show control bar to show the meeting controls. |
