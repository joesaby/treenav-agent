# Optimized Grid View in Low-Bandwidth for Cisco Webex Meetings

A low-bandwidth warning appears if your network connection falls below the minimum requirements for video. During low-bandwidth conditions, you may not be able to receive all the participant video streams. Earlier, the available streams appeared sporadically in the grid view. 

In 39.5 and later sites, video streams are prioritized to appear first in the grid view.
