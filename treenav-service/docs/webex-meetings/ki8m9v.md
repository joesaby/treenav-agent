# Share an announcement during a Q&A session in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

You can choose whether you want to show the announcement immediately or save it for later.

Before you begin

Make sure you've [turned on the Q&A feature in Slido](https://help.webex.com/jrpuyt/).

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to the Q&A tab in Slido, and then click Review.                                                                                                                         |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Create announcement ![Create announcement](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459150.jpg), and then type your announcement. |
| 3 | Click Announce now to send the announcement immediately. Click Save to activate it later.                                                                                                               |
| 4 | When you want to stop showing the announcement, click Stop announcement.                                                                                                                                |
