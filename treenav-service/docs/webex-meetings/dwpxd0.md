# Webex App | Join a call with audio only

- [iPhone, iPad, and Android](#id%5F83933)
- [Web](#id%5F93112)

| Tap ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/429001-430000/429940.jpg) for an audio call. You'll still see video from other people who have their video turned on, but they can't see you. In your meeting or call, you can switch back to a video call at any time by tapping ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450687.jpg) and selecting Switch to video call. |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| If you're joining from a web browser on your computer, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/432001-433000/432299.jpg). In the Video section, select No video. If you aren't showing your video, keep in mind that you won't be able to see anyone else's video either. In your meeting or call, you can switch back to a video call at any time by clicking ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427407.jpg). |
