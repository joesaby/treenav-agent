# Polls in Slido

## Polls in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

You can also create polls in webinars from the Polling panel, similar to Webex Meetings, even if Slido is not available in your webinar. For more information about polls that do not use Slido, see [Start a poll in Webex Meetings or Webex Webinars](https://help.webex.com/en-US/article/n0pdj9x/Start-a-poll-in-Webex-Meetings#task%5F6531E05B157C77BEEFF02E329A98FC3D).

Polls are a great way for meeting or webinar hosts to get input from participants. Use them to engage your audience, test knowledge, and ask for feedback.

As a meeting or webinar host, create and activate polls directly from Meetings by clicking Apps \> Slido.

If you want to create polls before the meeting or webinar, [add a guest collaborator](https://community.sli.do/setting-up-a-slido-event-82/share-access-and-add-guest-collaborators-to-your-event-501) to help you create and run the polls, go to <https://www.slido.com>, click Log In \> Log in with Webex, and enter your Webex username and password.

During the meeting or webinar, the host or guest collaborator can launch polls. Participants can view and answer the questions.

There are a few different [types of polls](https://help.webex.com/37pot3/) hosts can create, including single poll questions, quizzes, and surveys.

### Hosts

Here are the tasks you can perform as a host:

- [Create a poll](https://help.webex.com/007u2c/)
- [Create a survey](https://help.webex.com/qpvziw/)
- [Edit a poll](https://help.webex.com/ngvlwtu/)
- [Activate a poll](https://help.webex.com/b3l3kc/) or [activate a quiz](https://help.webex.com/1bf2c3/)
- [Reset a poll](https://help.webex.com/rk8p59/)
- [Export and share poll results after a meeting](https://help.webex.com/nues53h/)
- [Delete a poll](https://help.webex.com/r8oxz5/)

In case you only want to use the Q&A during your meeting or webinar, you can turn off polls. Go to <https://www.slido.com>, click Log In \> Log in with Webex.
