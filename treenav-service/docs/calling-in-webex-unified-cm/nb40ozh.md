# Send Email Messages to All Webex Site Users

| 1 | Sign in to Webex Site Administration and go to Configuration  \> Email  \> Send Email to All . Your name and email address appears in the sender information section. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Write your message and select Send . In your message to all users, do not include any variables that are in the email templates for your Webex service.               |
