# Reply to, archive, and restore questions in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Q&A in Slido, select a question in Audience Q&A, click ⋮, and then click Reply. If you don't want to reply to a question right now but you want to save it for later, click Archive.                     |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Type your reply, choose whether to send your reply from the Moderator or use your name, and then click Send.                                                                                                                                |
| 3 | Click ✓ Mark as answered  to move the question to the Archive.                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 4 | Restore a question by going to the Archive tab and clicking Restore![Restore](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459161.jpg). Questions reappear in the Audience Q&A. |
