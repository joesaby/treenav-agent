# Calls and the Cisco Room Phone

Use the Call button to place a call.

To make it easier for you, your last 25 calls are in your call history. Calling also includes predictive dialing, which is a list of generated suggestions that are based on your search. 

[Make a call](#task%5F00C746BFFDFA5FEDB297F38B14313388)

Your phone makes it easy to call a coworker or client. 

| 1 | Tap Call![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394166.jpg).                                                                                                                       |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Enter a phone number, a name, or select a call from the list of recent calls. Tap Search or dial to display the keypad. International calls require the plus (+) sign as the first character. Tap .?123 and select + before you enter the phone number. |
| 3 | (Optional) Enter a person's name and search from the directory. This feature is only available if your administrator configures it.                                                                                                                     |
| 4 | Tap Call. The phone LED lights up when the person accepts your call. A timer displays the length of the call. The call appears on your display screen if you use one.                                                                                   |
| 5 | Tap End call to finish your call.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |

[Answer a call](#task%5F852905C04BFBA44D888A6FF8E64D5D84)

Answer your phone when it is ringing, and start a discussion with another person. 

The phone LED lights up when you accept a call. A timer displays the length of the call and the call appears on your display screen.

| Do one of the following actions: Tap Answer.Tap Decline if you don't want to answer the call.Press Volume ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394170.jpg) down on your phone. The ring volume returns to normal with the next call. |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

[Adjust your call volume](#task%5F9C4AAAD1641DD4AE4B0D0482DBE921A4)

You can change your call volume to make it quieter or louder during your calls. This helps you hear the other person, and it helps others to hear you

When you adjust the call volume, it doesn't adjust the ring volume.

| Press Volume![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394170.jpg) right or left to adjust the call volume. |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

[Silence the incoming call ring](#task%5F541240ACC85FC44311B7D167F99260E9)

If you're busy or you don't want to be disturbed, then you can silence the incoming call ringer. The ring volume returns to normal with the next call.

| When you have an incoming call, press Volume ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394170.jpg) down |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

[Mute your audio](#task%5F1F5EB90184562021497C9BB0F8F70381)

While you are on a call, you can mute the audio. This allows you to hear the other person, but they can't hear you.

When you mute the audio, the LED bar lights red and the mute icon displays on the phone screen and connected HDMI display screen.

| 1 | Press Mute![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394169.jpg) on the phone or Mute on an expansion microphone. When you press Mute on an expansion microphone, the phone and all microphones are muted. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Press Mute again to turn mute off.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |

[Put a call on hold](#task%5F451A826FFF563DC3F7BFEC96EB7F98AB)

Put a call on hold if you want to talk privately or want to make a second call. You can have up to two held calls.

This feature is only available if your administrator configures it.

| 1 | Tap Hold.                              |
| - | -------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Resume to resume a call from hold. |

[Swap between active and held calls](#task%5FFBE7DB9D2BF0345C0864FEF84EAC1444)

You can easily switch between active and held calls. Your held calls display at the top of your phone screen. 

This feature is only available if your administrator configures it.

| Tap Swap to switch to the held call. Your active call moves to hold. |
