# Put a call on hold on your analog phone

You can put an active call on hold and then resume the call when you're ready.

| 1 | While on an active call, perform a hook flash.                                                                            |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | To return to the call, either: Perform another hook flash.Hang up the handset. Pick up the handset when your phone rings. |
