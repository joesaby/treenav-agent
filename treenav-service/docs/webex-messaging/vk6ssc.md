- [Android](#task%5F8F744DD2B7486CBE18439CB93B54D23D)

You can also go to the activity menu ![More options button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/461001-462000/461853.jpg) in the space, tap Content and select the file that you want to share and then tap ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/429001-430000/429639.jpg). |

| 2 | Choose Webex from the available options. Tap Choose Space to search for and select the new space. Then, enter your message and tap Post. If you don’t see Webex App in the list, tap more ![](../../../../../../../en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438337.JPG), and add it to the sharing options. You can also share files with other apps on your device, just select them from the available options list.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
