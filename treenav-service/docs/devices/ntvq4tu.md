# Check room availability and room schedule with Webex Share

When you walk into a room that has a Webex Share, look at the TV screen for the calendar.

If you don’t see the TV screen displaying the calendar, then your organization’s administration has not enabled the feature.

If the top bar of the calendar is blue, that means the room is available. If top bar of the calendar is orange, that means the room is reserved.

The status of a room may change from ‘Available’ to ‘Reserved’ if someone schedules a meeting.

 To reserve a room that has a Webex Share, see [Schedule a Meeting with Cisco Webex Share](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/en-us/cvdltq).
