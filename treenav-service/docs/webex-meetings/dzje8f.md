# Raise your hand in Webex Meetings, Webex Webinars, and Webex Events (classic)

If you don't see the option to raise your hand, the host or cohost [may not allow participants to raise their hands](https://help.webex.com/en-US/article/n4zrz0bb/).

- [Desktop](#task%5FFB244EB9809841CC4D3455E1DD05908D)
- [Mobile](#id%5F138161)

| Click ![Raise Hand](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455214.jpg) next to Reactions. Lower your hand by clicking ![Raise Hand](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455214.jpg) again. |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

How you raise and lower your hand depends on your device. 

| For iOS and Android phones, tap More options ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450687.jpg), then tap Raise hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455214.jpg) or Lower hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455215.jpg). For iPads and Android tablets, tap Reactions ![Clicking on the reactions button.](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/452001-453000/452811.jpg), then tap Raise hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455214.jpg) or Lower hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455215.jpg). If you're using an iPad or Android tablet and [reactions](https://help.webex.com/kb5tpo/) are turned off for your meeting, tap Raise hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455214.jpg) or Lower hand ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455215.jpg) from the meeting controls. |
