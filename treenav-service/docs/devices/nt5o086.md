# Charge your Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980

## Charge your Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980

Use the included USB-C cable to charge your B&O Cisco 980\. It takes about 2.5 hours to fully charge the headset. As your headset charges, the right LED slowly pulses to show the battery status. When your headset is fully charged, the power LED changes from pulsing green to solid green.

__Table 1\. Headset Charge Status__
| Power LED     | Charge Status |
| ------------- | ------------- |
| Solid green   | Full Charge   |
| Blinks green  | High charge   |
| Blinks yellow | Medium charge |

| 1 | Insert the USB-C cable into the USB-C charging port at the bottom of the right ear cup. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Plug the USB-A end of the cable into any powered USB port.                              |
