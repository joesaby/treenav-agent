# Start a Scheduled Webex Meeting by Phone

Before you begin

To start a meeting by phone, you need to [save the phone number](https://help.webex.com/ntwtdhl/) Webex Meetings uses to authenticate you, [set a host PIN](https://help.webex.com/en-us/ntwtdhl/), and [schedule your meeting with Webex Audio set as your connection type](https://help.webex.com/bx9si3/). 

| 1 | Open the email invite you received when you scheduled the meeting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Under More ways to join, dial either the toll or toll-free number that is listed.                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| 3 | Enter the access code or the meeting number under Join by meeting number in the email invitation and press #.                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 4 | When prompted, enter your host PIN and press #. If you're calling from a phone that doesn't match the phone number that is saved in your Webex Audio and Video preferences, you're prompted to enter the saved phone number and press #. Then, you can enter your host PIN and press #. |
