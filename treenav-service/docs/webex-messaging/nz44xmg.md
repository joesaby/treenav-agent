# Webex App | Hide less important spaces

You can hide all types of spaces that you no longer find important, including group spaces and direct message spaces.

- [Desktop](#id%5F120703)
- [iOS](#Cisco%5FTask.dita%5F1d84af95-e4b2-4fff-ad45-34f4598085c6)
- [Android](#id%5F128966)

| In the spaces list, right-click the space and select Hide. Or, when you're in the space, go to Space Information ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427579.jpg), click Notifications, select Off and Hide Space, then click Save. When you hide a space: All notifications are automatically turned off for that space. If the space is in your favorite list, it's removed from the list. However, you're still able to see hidden spaces in search results, and in your team's list of spaces. To unhide a space go to ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439461.jpg), select Hidden, then right-click the space and select Unhide. |

When you hide a space: All notifications are automatically turned off for that space. If the space is in your favorite list, it's removed from the list. However, you're still able to see hidden spaces in search results, and in your team's list of spaces. To unhide a space tap ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/461001-462000/461851.jpg), select Hidden, you can then either long press on the space and tap Unhide, or swipe left on the space, tap More, and then tap Unhide. |
