# Reset a Webex Share to the factory settings

If you want to reset a Webex Share to its default settings, you can use the factory reset button.

Performing a factory reset clears all data and settings from the Webex Share permanently. After performing a factory reset, you will have to register the Webex Share again, with a new activation code.
