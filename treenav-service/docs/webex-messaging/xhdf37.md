# Webex App | Call someone using their name, email, or video address

If your administrator adds external contacts, these contacts appear in search results.

- [Desktop and Web](#task%5Fv1n%5F4yk%5Fqz)
- [Mobile](#id%5F120449)

You can use the app header in Webex App to call someone else who uses the app. The system automatically dials the phone number assigned to the contact. If there's not a phone number to dial, the assigned email address is used.

| On your desktop app, click ![Search icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/454001-455000/454733.jpg) **Search, meet, and call**, type the name, email address, or video address, and then click ![Audio Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/429001-430000/429940.jpg) to make an audio call, or ![Video Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438668.jpg) to make a video call. On your web app, click ![Add button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/444001-445000/444067.jpg) in the app header, and then select Make a call. You see this option if you have a Webex Calling plan. If you're set up with a phone service such as Unified CM, Webex Calling, or Calling hosted by a Service Provider, you also have access to a dial pad and can make a call from there. Just click ![Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/417001-418000/417356.jpg) and enter a person's name, email address, or video address (such as for a room device) in the dial pad. For a better understanding of how your desk phone can interact with Webex App, see [Make Webex calls from your desk phone](https://help.webex.com/en-us/5mgmmb/). |
