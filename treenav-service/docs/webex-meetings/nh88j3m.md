# Rotate your video in Webex Meetings

| 1 | Click Video Options![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450681.jpg) in the control bar and select Settings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Rotate ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/440001-441000/440775.jpg) in the video preview until the video orientation displays correctly. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455072.jpg) If you're using a virtual background, it will not display upside down or sideways. Rotating the video display does not rotate the virtual background. |
| 3 | Click Apply.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
