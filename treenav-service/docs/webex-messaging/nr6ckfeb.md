# Webex App | Box integration

## Add your account

Get access to your company's content management system without leaving Webex App. Share and edit files in a space with another person, or with your team. 

- Enterprise accounts––Your administrator gives you permissions to access your company's enterprise content management system.

**Already have permissions?** [Add your Box account](https://help.webex.com/article/nmedqv7) to your desktop or mobile app.
- Service provider accounts––Contact your provider to get access to your company's content management system.
- Account administrators–– [Provision content management](https://help.webex.com/en-us/article/na9del2/Provision-content-management#concept%5Fbb4%5Frt4%5Ftsb).

## Upload a file

[Upload files to Box](https://help.webex.com/article/nd6xe3f) on the desktop app, so you're ready to share them in a space. Limit access to view-only if you're sharing informational content, allow editing permissions for team collaboration and discussions.

## Share a file

[Share files from Box](https://help.webex.com/article/nufbfgu) in a space from the desktop or mobile app and start collaborating with your team.

## View and edit a file

[View and edit files shared from Box](https://help.webex.com/article/nmqsb8f) depending on the shared permissions. The file opens in the application that's associated with the file type on your desktop or mobile.

## Collaborate on a file

Start or schedule meetings in your Personal Room to [collaborate on files with Box](https://help.webex.com/en-us/article/o7wrc7/). Switch effortlessly from file preview to a live meeting with your file collaborators.
