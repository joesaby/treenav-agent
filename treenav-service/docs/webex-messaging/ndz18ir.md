# Webex App bots

## Bots

_We are migrating our bots to a new platform to align with updated architectural requirements. As part of this transition, you will need to manually recreate your existing bot notifications, as, unfortunately, there is no automated way to transfer them. We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience and are here to support you throughout the process. If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team._

- **[Azure DevOps Services bot](https://help.webex.com/article/edxa3db/)**  
The Azure DevOps Services bot one can easily stay on top of the latest changes happening in team’s repositories. Users can receive customizable notifications such as Pull Request, Release Deployment, Work Item, Build Completed, Code Checked In and more.
- **Outlook Alerts for Microsoft Office bot**  
The Outlook Alerts for Microsoft Office 365 bot for Webex lets you closely monitor when a new email is received, meeting is added to your calendar, or a folder is created in your inbox so that you never miss out on important updates. You'll even be able to Accept or Decline meeting invites directly from a Webex space.
- **[Github Cloud bot](https://help.webex.com/article/nx5tvdu/)**  
With GitHub Cloud one can streamline code collaboration and receive notifications for Commit push, Pull Request, Branch, Release, Repository, Issue , Wiki Page Updates.
- **[Jenkins bot](https://help.webex.com/article/8ioisl/)**  
With the Jenkins bot for Webex, you get instant updates when code is built and ready to deploy. One can get customizable notifications for Job Queued, Job Completed, Job Started, Job Finalized.
- **[Google Calendar bot](https://help.webex.com/article/q2rx40/)**  
With the Google Calendar bot one can receive handy reminders before next event starts, or find out when your teammates update shared invites—right when the changes happen. Receive customizable notifications for Event Created, Event Reminder Posted, Daily Reminder Posted.
- **Birthday bot**  
Recognize and keep track of your team member's birthdays, and celebrate in Webex App. Add the Birthday bot to any space and ask it to capture everyone’s birthdays, for a celebratory message on their special day.
- **[Jira Cloud bot](https://help.webex.com/article/l2th86/)**  
Jira helps users plan, track, and manage software development projects. The Jira cloud bot allows you to stay up to date with your Jira projects directly in Webex App.
- **[Smartsheet bot](https://help.webex.com/article/khr1bv/)**  
Smartsheet helps users stay organized and communicate throughout projects. The Smartsheet bot helps you stay up to date and quickly make changes to a sheet directly from Webex App.
- **[Watercooler bot](https://help.webex.com/article/nxe8hu8/)**  
The Watercooler bot is the perfect way to encourage team bonding and breaking the ice between teammates. Add the bot to any group space in Webex App and it takes care of the rest, pairing new teammates daily, weekly, or even monthly to keep the conversations going!
- **[PagerDuty bot](https://help.webex.com/article/szppp/)**  
PagerDuty helps teams all over the world proactively manage and respond to business-critical incidents. When connected to Webex App, users can automate incident management and come together to resolve issues even faster.
- **[Asana bot](https://help.webex.com/article/ndcgxz0/)**  
Asana helps users stay up to date and organized with their projects using real-time notifications. The Asana bot provides the ability to create, update, and search for tasks and subtasks directly from Webex App.
