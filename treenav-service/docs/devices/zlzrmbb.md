# Connect the Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 3.5mm cable

## Connect the Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 3.5mm cable

Use the included 3.5 mm audio cable to connect your headset with your laptop or mobile device. You can use the audio cable to make calls and listen to music on a non-wireless device.

You can't control calls or music playback when you use the 3.5 mm cable. Any connected Bluetooth® sources will have priority over the 3.5 mm audio connection.

| 1 | Plug the 3.5 mm audio cable into the port at the bottom of the right ear cup. |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Insert the other end of the cable into the 3.5 mm jack on your device.        |
