# Webex App | Quickly start a meeting

| 1 | In the app header, click ![Add button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/444001-445000/444067.jpg), and then select Start a Meeting.                                                                                   |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Start Meeting. In the meeting, go to Meeting Info, and choose from: Copy meeting link—copy the meeting link to share it directly with people or spaces in Webex App. Invite and remind—enter the email address or name of each person you want to invite, and click Send. |
