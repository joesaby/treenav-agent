| 1 | Click your profile picture, select Settings \> Integrations, and then click Add Account.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | In Add an account, choose Box.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 3 | An external browser will open.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 4 | Enter your account information and connect to your Box account.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 5 | Click Grant access to Box.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 6 | You may be prompted by your browser to allow Box to open the Webex App. Click Yes. Your administrator can restrict the account that you use to sign into your Box account. If you receive an access error, contact your administrator to confirm your account information. You can follow the same steps to sign in to Box by clicking on a shared Box file in a Webex space or choosing the paperclip icon, Share from Box. |
