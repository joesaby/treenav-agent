# Webex App | Join a meeting as a guest

This article applies to meetings on the [Webex Suite meeting platform](https://help.webex.com/m61d8eb/).

You can also [click the meeting link in your email or calendar invite](https://help.webex.com/ga1mli/) to join quickly.

If you prefer not to join as a guest, there are other ways to [join a meeting](https://help.webex.com/k7cstu/).

If the meeting host requires you to sign in to your Webex account, you'll see a message letting you know. Sign in to join the meeting.

- [Desktop](#id%5F138422)
- [Mobile](#task-template%5F0dbafcf0-81eb-4ae2-93b2-d6b3c1cb77fb)
- [Web](#task-template%5F7a9beae7-fb40-4824-b228-ed221e8e3e8b)

| 1 | Open Webex App and click Join a meeting.                                                                                                                 |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Enter the meeting number or link and click Next. Find the meeting number or link in the email or meeting invite that you received from the meeting host. |
| 3 | In the Name field, enter your name as you want it to appear in the meeting. Check Remember me to save time the next time you join a meeting.             |
| 4 | If required, enter your email address.                                                                                                                   |
| 5 | If you're joining a meeting on a Webex site with [CAPTCHA enabled](https://help.webex.com/qfb19w/), enter the CAPTCHA.                                   |
| 6 | [Adjust your audio and video settings](https://help.webex.com/ela6i8/) before you join.                                                                  |
| 7 | Click Join meeting.                                                                                                                                      |
