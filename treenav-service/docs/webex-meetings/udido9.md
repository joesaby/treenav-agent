# Webex App | Choose your call me phone numbers

Then, it'll be easy to select which one of the saved phone numbers you want the meeting to call when you choose Call me.

- [Windows](#Cisco%5FTask%5Fin%5FList%5FGUI.dita%5Fbc1df5da-1355-401b-aaa0-4e2c17cf4fcd)
- [Mac](#Cisco%5FTask%5Fin%5FList%5FGUI.dita%5Fd4712b16-ce68-4795-b091-c3250dc3d22f)

| 1 | [Open settings from your profile picture](https://help.webex.com/m67xht/).                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select Join options.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 3 | For Call me phone numbers, select Manage my Call me phone numbers. ![Call me phone numbers](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/454001-455000/454973.jpg) The app opens the Audio and Video preferences page on your Webex site. |
| 4 | For My phone numbers, enter your phone number and check Show in Call me numbers list. Repeat for each phone number that you want to show in the list.                                                                                                                                   |
| 5 | Click Save.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
