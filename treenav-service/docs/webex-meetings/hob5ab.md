# Turn off your computer's microphone when you're not in a Webex Meeting

- [Windows](#id%5F122385)
- [Mac](#id%5F122386)

| 1 | Sign in to your Meetings desktop app, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438751.jpg) and then select Preference  \> Video system and uncheck  Automatically discover nearby devices. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439667.jpg) |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | When you're ready to [connect to a video device](https://help.webex.com/en-us/nqx2ohdb/Use-the-Cisco-Webex-Meetings-Desktop-App#id%5F93254) to share content or connect to meetings, just turn on the setting again. For more information on disabling your computer's microphone, refer to the computer manufacturer's instructions.                                                         |
