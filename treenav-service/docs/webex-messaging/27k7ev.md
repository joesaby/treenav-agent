# Webex App | Share link to your Personal Room

Your Personal Room must be associated with Webex App before you can add your Personal Room link to a message. See [Webex App | Set up your Personal Room ](https://help.webex.com/e7l52d/) for more information.

| When writing a message, select My Personal Room invite. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/424001-425000/424780.jpg) Your Personal Room link is inserted in the message. You don't have to type it. Here's an example: https://company.webex.com/meet/acassidy |
