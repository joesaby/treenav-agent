# Toggle name labels on and off on Board, Desk, and Room series device

At the start of a meeting, you can view the names of up to four people on the far-end for the first 60 seconds. Afterward, the name of the person currently speaking briefly appears when they start speaking.

If your organization allows name labels, you can toggle them on or off on your device. When you turn off name labels, they won't appear during your next call. However, if name labels are available, you will still receive them. After the call ends, the name labels are automatically turned back on.

Name labels are not available on transcoded conferences or on soft clients. 

| 1 | Open the settings menu by swiping right on the home screen, and select Device Settings .           |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Face recognition name labels  and tap the toggle to turn the labels on or off for your device. |
