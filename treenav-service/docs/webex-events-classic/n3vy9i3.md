# View Participants with Their Hands Raised in Cisco Webex Events

| 1 | Select Participants ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450689.jpg) to open the Participants  panel.                                                                                 |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click View all attendees  in the Participants  panel. ![View all attendees](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/452001-453000/452494.jpg)                                                              |
| 3 | In the Attendee List , click Feedback  to move the attendees who raised their hands or gave feedback to the top of the list. ![Feedback](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/452001-453000/452496.jpg) |
