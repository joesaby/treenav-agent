# Unlink Google Home devices from the Webex Meetings mobile app

| 1 | Open Google Assistant and tap Explore![Explore icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438093.jpg).                             |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | In the search bar, enter Webex, and then tap the Webex result.                                                                                                                                         |
| 3 | Tap Unlink. If you want to complete any actions that require account linking, such as joining a meeting from a Google Home device, you can [link your accounts](https://help.webex.com/p1j4si/) again. |
