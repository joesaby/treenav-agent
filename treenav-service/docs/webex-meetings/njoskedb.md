# Switch your phone’s audio in the Webex Meetings mobile app

| 1 | Open the Meetings app on your mobile device and tap Sign In.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Tap Start Meeting![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502391.jpg).                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 3 | Tap the Bluetooth icon ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/393001-394000/393177.jpg) to switch between Bluetooth, phone, and speaker while in the meeting. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/448001-449000/448410.jpg) |
