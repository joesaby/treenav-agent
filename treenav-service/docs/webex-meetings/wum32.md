# Pop panels in and out in Webex meetings

| 1 | In your meeting, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459973.jpg) to float the panel in its own window. Once the panel is floating, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459972.jpg) to pop the panel back into the meeting window at any time, and it will snap back into place. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click X in the meeting window to close any floating panels. If you close a floating panel, you can reopen it from the meeting window.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
