# Download a recording transcript for meetings and webinars

This feature is only available on video-centric MP4 recordings.

| 1 | Sign in to [User Hub](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin), go to Recordings, and click Meeting.                                                                                                           |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Choose the recording that you want, then click Download![Download](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438843.jpg)in the Transcript panel. |
