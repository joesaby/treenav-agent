# Webex App | Find out what calling service you have

- [Desktop](#id%5F133572)
- [Mobile](#id%5F133574)

| Click Settings ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427579.jpg) on the navigation sidebar, then go to Phone Services. You'll see one of the following services here: You're signed in to Cisco Unified Communications Manager—Your calling service is Unified CM. You're signed in to Webex Calling—Your calling service is Webex Calling. You're signed in—Your calling service is hosted by your Service Provider. If you don't see a Phone Services setting, that means your organization is configured for Webex App calls only. For information about what features are available to you, see [Supported calling options](https://help.webex.com/xga73p/) |
