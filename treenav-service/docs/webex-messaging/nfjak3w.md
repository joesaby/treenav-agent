# Webex App | See when you've been added to a new space

| Take a look at the Spaces list to see the ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/429001-430000/429300.jpg) icon next to the new spaces. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/439001-440000/439912.jpg) Before you join the space, hover over the space icon to see the space contact card. You can check out who is in the space and find out who added you to the space. If you don’t see the notification, check that you haven’t turned off notifications for all spaces. See [Set Notifications for All Spaces](https://help.webex.com/nmoypobb/) for more details. |
