# Set up integration connectors for Webex Contact Center

## Availability of connectors

- **Webex Contact Center** platform supports Webex Contact Center HTTP, Salesforce, Google, Google CCAI, Webex Experience Management, Custom connectors.
- **Webex Contact Center 1.0** platform supports Salesforce and Custom connectors.

## Configure a Connector

You can configure the following connectors on the Control Hub to integrate the third-party applications with the Webex Contact Center.

- [Webex Contact Center HTTP](https://help.webex.com/article/n54f5wd/)
- [Google CCAI](https://help.webex.com/article/9zvadfb)
- [Google TTS](https://help.webex.com/article/ctge8i)
- [Webex Experience Management](https://help.webex.com/article/j6ul1k)
- [Custom](https://help.webex.com/article/n4u702ab)
- [Salesforce](https://help.webex.com/article/t2xs2ab)
- [Microsoft Dynamics 365](https://help.webex.com/en-us/article/460fet/)
- [Microsoft Teams](https://help.webex.com/article/n1sztxbb/Integrate-Webex-Contact-Center-with-Microsoft-Teams)

## Edit a Connector

| 1 | Log in to your customer organization at <https://admin.webex.com> and navigate to Services \> Contact Center \> Tenant Settings \> Integrations \> Connectors.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click the connector name that appears on the card. To refresh the Google Contact Center AI connector authentication key, click Refresh Key and reauthenticate with Google. Ensure that you disable the Pop-up Blocker on your browser.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| 3 | You must first deactivate a connector before you can edit fields in the Credentials section. Click Deactivate and confirm. When you deactivate a connector, the scripts that are currently in execution may work for some time if the credentials are valid. After you deactivate a connector, you can either reactivate, edit, or delete the connector. Edit the fields as required and click Reactivate. You cannot modify the Connector ID. You must reauthenticate the Google Contact Center AI connector with Google. Click Delete to delete the connector. You cannot delete the Google Contact Center AI connector if there is a CCAI Config associated with it. |
