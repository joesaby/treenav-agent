# Turn your headset on and off

## Turn your headset on and off

The Power/Bluetooth switch is located on the back of the left ear cup. 

| 1 | Slide the Power/Bluetooth switch up to turn on the headset.    |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Slide the Power/Bluetooth switch down to turn off the headset. |
