# Webex App | Mark up shared content with annotations

If you're the host or cohost and want to keep the presentation focused, [turn off annotation](https://help.webex.com/xr53yg/) to prevent attendees from requesting to annotate.

- [Windows](#id%5F82167-windows)
- [Mac](#task%5F69CB04474715373E92017A3AE4F8C8FD)
- [iOS](#task%5F10265E392465648F97A18D45F664A44D)
- [Android](#id%5F82167)
- [Web](#id%5F97763)

This feature is available on our Webex Suite meeting platform. [Find out if your meetings use the Webex Suite meeting platform](https://help.webex.com/m61d8eb/).

Some [calls in Webex App](https://help.webex.com/article/z94f4f) have these features too.

| 2 | You can choose from the following annotation tools to start marking up the annotation: OptionDescription ![drag the panel](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475350.svg) Allows you to move the annotation panel within the meeting interface for convenient access to annotation tools. ![Whiteboard pointer](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/469001-470000/469814.jpg) Cursor—Allows you to control your screen and move your mouse pointer. It doesn’t make any changes or annotations on the screen. ![whiteboard laser pointer button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/480001-490000/489001-490000/489860.svg) Laser pointer—Allows you to draw or write on the screen using laser ink. However, the ink automatically disappears after a short period of time, allowing you to temporarily highlight or emphasize something without permanently marking the screen. ![arrow pointer button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/480001-490000/489001-490000/489872.svg) Arrow pointer—Allows you to point out exactly what you're discussing. Click anywhere on the screen, and your arrow pointer moves to that spot, making it easy for others to follow along. Each participant has one arrow pointer. Your arrow pointer has your name on it, and you can choose a color for it. When you click anywhere on the screen (except the far left), the arrow points to the right. If you click on the leftmost part of the screen, the arrow points to the left. To clear your pointer, click Clear ![clean up](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475357.svg) \> Clear my pointer. If you're the presenter, click Clear ![clean up](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475357.svg) \> Clear all pointers to remove all the pointers from the shared content. ![Whiteboard pen](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/469001-470000/469816.jpg) Pen—Allows you to draw or write on the screen using laser ink. The strokes made with the pen tool stay on the screen until they are manually cleared or saved. ![Whiteboard text](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/469001-470000/469820.jpg) Text—Allows you to use your keyboard to type rather than draw text with your mouse. ![Whiteboard eraser](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/469001-470000/469818.jpg) Eraser—Allows you to remove or erase any annotations or strokes made on the screen. You can select the eraser and then click on an annotation to remove it. ![undo](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475355.svg) Undo—Allows you to reverse or undo the last annotation or action performed. It helps you correct any mistakes or remove an annotation you didn't intend to make. ![redo](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475356.svg) Redo—Allows you to reapply an annotation or action that was previously undone using the undo tool. It’s used to restore an annotation or action that was mistakenly removed. ![clean up](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475357.svg) Clear—Provides options to remove annotations from the screen. It includes the following suboptions: Clear my pointer: Removes your arrow pointer from the shared content.Clear all pointers: Removes all the arrow pointers from the shared content. This option is available to the presenter only. Clear all annotations: Removes all annotations made by any participant. Clear my annotations: Removes only the annotations made by you. Clear other participants' annotations: Removes the annotations made by other participants, but not your own. ![save](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475358.svg) Save—Allows you to capture a screenshot of the screen with all the annotations and save it to your Downloads folder. ![Settings button represented by a cog icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438264.jpg) Annotation privileges—Allows you to manage the annotation permissions for participants. It includes the following options: Anyone can annotate: Allows all participants to make annotations on the screen without any approval. Others need to ask for approval: Participants must request permission to make annotations, and the host or presenter can approve or deny the requests. Block all requests to annotate: Disables annotation privileges for all participants. ![cancel](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/475001-476000/475359.svg) Stop annotating—Provides options to stop or cancel the annotation session. |
