# Add labels to questions in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Q&A in Slido.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Hover over the selected question, click the ⋯ \> Labels                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 3 | Enter a name for the label and click Create new label  or use an existing one. If you need to delete a label, click the bin icon next to the label.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 4 | (Optional) You can [enable the labels for participants](https://community.slido.com/audience-q-a-42/assign-questions-to-speakers-using-labels-517#Labels+for+participants) at [slido.com](https://slido.com). Then, ask your participants to indicate which topic their question relates to by adding a label to it. After you and your participants add labels to questions, you can [filter questions by label](https://help.webex.com/1yh9vx/). |
