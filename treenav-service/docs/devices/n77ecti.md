# Turn your presence LED lights on and off

## Turn your presence LED lights on and off

You can manually activate the presence LED lights when you aren't on an active call. When you are on a call, the presence LED lights automatically show solid red.

| 1 | Hold Play to turn the presence LED lights on.                                                                                                                                                 |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Hold Play again to turn the lights off. ![Cisco Headset 730 play button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/440001-441000/440489.jpg) |
