# Deregister a Hybrid Services Resource from the Cloud

Before you begin

- Any hybrid services that are running on the resource must be deactivated first.
- You can only perform this procedure from Control Hub, not directly on the hybrid services resource interface.

| 1 | From the customer view in <https://admin.webex.com>, go to Services \> Hybrid, click Viewfrom the On-Premises Resources card, and then click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/414001-415000/414887.jpg) from a resource name in the list. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Scroll to Cluster, and then click **Remove**. After you read and understand the confirmation prompt and you're sure you want to proceed, click **Confirm**.                                                                                                                                             |

What to do next

You can readd a resource for hybrid services if you change your mind, but you need to reconfigure its settings and reenable Webex App users for hybrid services.
