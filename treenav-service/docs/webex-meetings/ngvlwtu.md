# Edit a poll in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Apps \> Slido or click ⋯ \> Slido (polling and Q&A) if you're using Webex Suite meeting platform.                                                                                             |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select the Slido that contains the poll to edit. If you select the wrong Slido, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459183.jpg) \> Recent Slidos. |
| 3 | Select your poll, then click ⋯ \> Edit. Make your changes, and Launch the poll imediately or go back to the list of interactions.                                                                                                |
