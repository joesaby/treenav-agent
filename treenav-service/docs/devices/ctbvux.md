# Call control for Cisco Headset 520 and 530 Series

## Make and answer calls

When you have an incoming call, you hear the ringtone in your headset.

| On the inline controller, press Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg). |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Adjust your volume

| PressUp![up](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425660.jpg) or Down![down](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425661.jpg) on the inline controller. |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Mute yourself on a call

| Press Mute ![the mute button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425656.jpg) on the inline controller. |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Place a call on hold

| Press Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) once on the inline controller. If you have an incoming call, the headset answers automatically. |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## End a call

| Hold Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) on the inline controller until you hear a tone. |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Reject a call

| Press Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) twice on the inline controller. |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Place a Call on Hold and Resume a Held Call

| 1 | Press Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) on the inline controller. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select the call you want to resume.                                                                                                                                          |
| 3 | Press Call.                                                                                                                                                                  |

## End a Call and Answer an Incoming Call

| 1 | Hold Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) until you hear a tone. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Press Call once.                                                                                                                                                         |

## End a Call and Resume a Held Call

| 1 | Hold Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/425001-426000/425658.jpg) until you hear a tone. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | On your phone, select the call that you want to resume.                                                                                                                  |
| 3 | Press Call once.                                                                                                                                                         |

## Turn your headset on and off

| 1 | Hold Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/393001-394000/393941.jpg) on your headset for 4 seconds. You hear a tone indicating headset status.                        |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Do one of these actions: Hold Call ![the call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/393001-394000/393941.jpg) for 1 second to turn the headset back on.Place the headset on the base. |
