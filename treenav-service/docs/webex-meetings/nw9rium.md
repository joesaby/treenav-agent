| 2 | Select Names in videos and then choose one of the following options: Automatically hide names when not speaking—Default option that keeps names hidden until the participant speaks. Show all names—Shows participant names always. Hide all names—Hides participant names except for when you hover over their video. |

| To show or hide all participant names during a meeting, tap Layout ![Layout icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455202.jpg), then toggle Automatically hide names on or off. |
