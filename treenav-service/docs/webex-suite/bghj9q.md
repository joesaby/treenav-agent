# Enable Remember Me for Users in Webex Control Hub

This setting applies to all users in your organization.

| 1 | Sign in to [Control Hub](https://admin.webex.com/).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Go to Management \> Organization settings \> Settings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 3 | Scroll to the Remember Me Controls section.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 4 | Toggle Allow Remember Me controls on.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 5 | Set the number of days before users have to reenter their email address. The browser sets a cookie, containing the hashed email address, to expire at the end of the configured period.Users can clear their Webex browser cookies if they want to sign in with a different email address. |
