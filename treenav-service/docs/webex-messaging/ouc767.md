# Webex App | Hide your availability and status from specific people

Your availability and status is on by default. You can choose to [stop sharing your availability and status](https://help.webex.com/nkzs6wl/) with everyone. 

When you hide your availability and status from specific people, you'll see  next to the person's name in the message area.
