# Access and adjust selfview on Board, Desk, and Room Series

Your selfview shows what others see from your Board, Desk, or Room Series device in a call. You can use it to confirm that they see what you want them to see.

You may want to have your selfview activated during the meeting to ensure that you remain seen on the screen.

If you find the current position of the selfview blocks important parts of the image on your screen, you can move it around.

To check your selfview at any time, swipe from the right side of your device's home screen or tap the button. As long as your camera is open/on, you'll see your selfview in the control panel. 

There are icons on the side of the selfview. Tapping them, you can:

- Toggle Full screenon and off.
- Open the options where you can select to Show selfview or not, turn Full screen on and off, and select the onscreen Selfview position.
- Change the background that appears behind you.
- When your selfview is in fullscreen mode, you can also tap the camera button and adjust the camera view.

To close the control panel, tap anywhere else on the screen. 

If you have your selfview in fullscreen mode in a call, you can see the other participants on the top of the screen.

To open and change the location of your selfview on the screen during a call:

1. If it's not already visible, tap the screen to open the selfview up on top of the call.
2. Press the selfview and drag and drop it to change the location. It will snap to pre-defined locations as you drag.

To check your selfview at any time, swipe from the right side or tap the button on the controller's home screen. As long as your camera is open/on, you'll see your selfview on the device's screen. Tap Camera on the controller and then select Selfview. Here, you can:

- Toggle Show selfview on and off.
- Toggle Full screen on and off.
- Adjust the selfview position by tapping any of the predefined positions shown on the touch controller screen.

If you have your selfview in fullscreen mode in a call, you can see the other participants on the top of the screen.

To open and change the location of your selfview on the screen during a call: 

1. Tap the arrow at the bottom of the controller's home screen to get the call controls. Tap Selfview.
2. You get the same options as above, to set visibility, move, or change your selfview to full screen. Tap outside the camera options screen to return to the call controls.
