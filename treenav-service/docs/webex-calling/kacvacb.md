# Webex App | Voicemail

## Set up your PIN

Depending on the [calling service you're set up with](https://help.webex.com/nbtgrzx/), you may require a PIN to access your voice messages or features, such as creating a personalized greeting, sending a message, and more. The way you go about setting up your PIN, depends on your calling service:

- Unified CM— [Webex App | Set a voicemail PIN](https://help.webex.com/myh7e9/)
- Webex Calling or Calling hosted by a Service Provider— [Set or reset your voicemail PIN](https://help.webex.com/nbhhvl9/)

## Listen to your messages

You can listen to your voice messages right from the app and what you do with them after that depends on your calling service. Check out [Listen to or read your voice messages](https://help.webex.com/8u5983/) for more information.
