# Webex App | Switch between shared content and speaker in a call or meeting

- [Windows and Mac](#task%5F940F04435F52D1D46EF551F2C4D8F6E8)
- [Web](#id%5F84801)

When you join a meeting in Webex App and someone shares content, the shared content appears in the center of the screen and video of the meeting participants appears at the top of the screen.

If the meeting already has 20 people, anyone else who joins sees the shared content and the active speaker's video appear side by side.

| 1 | Select Zoom view ![Zoom view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/423001-424000/423835.jpg) to see the shared content in full-screen view. If the thumbnail videos are covering the shared content, drag the floating panel to another location on the screen.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select Swap shared content view with video view ![Swap shared content view with video view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/455001-456000/455622.jpg) in the floating panel to switch focus between the shared content and the active speaker's video. Select Hide video view ![Hide video view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/457001-458000/457816.jpg) in the floating panel to hide all thumbnails videos. The name of the active speaker shows in the floating panel. ![Active speaker's name shows only](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/457001-458000/457817.jpg) Select Focus view ![Focus view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/457001-458000/457818.jpg) in the floating panel to show the active speaker's video only. ![Active speaker's video](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/460001-461000/460090.jpg) Select Grid view ![Grid view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/457001-458000/457820.jpg) in the floating panel to show the videos of the meeting participants. ![Grid view](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/460001-470000/460001-461000/460134.jpg) Resize the floating panel to see more of the participants or more of the shared content during a meeting. Move your cursor over any edge of the panel until an arrow appears, and then drag to make the panel larger or smaller. |
