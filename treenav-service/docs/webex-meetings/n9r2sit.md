# Download your Webex recordings

The standard format for all recordings is MP4.

Anyone that you share the recording link with can [play and download it from the player view](https://help.webex.com/article/pzh5sw/). Share it with attendees so they can refer back to the meeting, or with people who couldn't attend.

| 1 | Sign in to [User Hub](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin).                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | Select Recordings from the navigation panel.                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 3 | Click Download![Download](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438843.jpg) next to the recording that you want to download. If a disclaimer appears, select Accept to accept the terms and continue. |
