# Webex App | 6 Special things about general spaces

1. Every team includes the General space, and you can't rename it (even if you're a team moderator).
2. When [team moderators](https://help.webex.com/xuwad5/) add you to a team, you automatically get added to the General space.
3. Only team moderators can add you to General, because you get added to the team too.
4. You can't move General out of its associated team.
5. When you leave the General space, you leave the team.
6. Team moderators can convert the General space to an [announcements-only](https://help.webex.com/n0wbhrz/) space. This way, moderators can use the space to share important information, and everyone might be more likely to post messages in topic-specific spaces.
