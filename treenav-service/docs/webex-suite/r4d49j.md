# End-of-Life Announcement for Care Assistant

Cisco announced the deprecation of Care Assistant feature. Care Assistant is a virtual assistant functionality provided for Webex Teams users. The functionality will not be available after **30th June 2020**. Customers with active use of the functionality are requested to explore and migrate to alternative solutions.
