# Webex App | Move between your visited spaces or tabs

There can be times when you will need to move from space to space, or tab to tab, and it can then be difficult to get back to where you were. The forward and back arrows in the app header let you quickly take a step back or jump forward, making it easy to revisit previously visited spaces.

| 1 | Go to the app header: Click Back ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458467.jpg) to go back to previous spaces or tabs that you’ve visited. Click Forward ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458468.jpg) to move forward through spaces or tabs that you’ve visited. |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click and hold Back ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458467.jpg) or Forward ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/458001-459000/458468.jpg) to view a history of the spaces or tabs that you've visited.                                                                           |
