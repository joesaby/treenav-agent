# Create a survey in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

You can create surveys or [create polls](https://help.webex.com/007u2c/) before or during a meeting or webinar. These instructions are for creating a survey during a meeting.

To create a survey before a meeting or webinar, go to <https://www.slido.com>, click Log In \> Log in with Webex, and enter your Webex username and password. Then, [create a poll, quiz, or a survey](https://community.sli.do/live-polls-quizzes-and-surveys-55/create-a-poll-quiz-or-a-survey-414). Surveys that you create on <https://www.slido.com> are listed for use in Meetings.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Apps \> Slido. In Webex Suite Platform click ⋯ \> Slido (polling and Q&A).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select an existing Slido or create a new one. When you create a Slido, it has the same name as the name of the meeting or webinar. For Personal Room meetings, the name format is _Meeting DD/MM/YYYY_.                                                                                                                                                         |
| 3 | Click \+ Add new interaction \> Survey \> \+ Add first question.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 4 | Choose the [type of poll](https://help.webex.com/37pot3/) to add as your first question.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| 5 | [Type your question and select the options](https://help.webex.com/007u2c/), if applicable.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 6 | Click + to add other poll questions to the survey.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 7 | Your survey is saved automatically and you can Launch it right away if preferred.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 8 | Test your survey: [Activate the survey](https://help.webex.com/b3l3kc/) and submit your responses. If necessary, [edit the survey](https://help.webex.com/ngvlwtu/). When you're finished testing, [reset the survey results](https://help.webex.com/rk8p59/). [Delete the survey](https://help.webex.com/r8oxz5/) if you created it for testing purposes only. |
