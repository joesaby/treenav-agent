# Webex App | Select a virtual camera for calls or meetings

| 1 | Join your meeting, then select ![Start video](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450406.jpg) or ![stop video](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450680.jpg).                                                                                         |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Under Camera , select your virtual camera from the list. _Mac users:_ Virtual cameras may or may not be enabled by your system administrator. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/452001-453000/452867.jpg) Camera options can also be managed prior to a call or meeting from your [video settings ](https://help.webex.com/lw2dx4/). |
