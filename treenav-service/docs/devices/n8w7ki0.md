# Connect the Cisco Headset 730 USB-C cable

## Connect the Cisco Headset 730 USB-C cable

Use the included USB-C cable to connect your headset with your laptop, Cisco IP phone, or Webex Desk device.

Audio through the USB-C cable will always take priority over other connected devices. Disconnect the USB-C cable if you want to switch to a Bluetooth® device.

| 1 | Plug the USB-C cable into the port at the bottom of the left ear cup. ![USB-C cable connecting to the bottom of the left ear cup.](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/300001-400000/390001-400000/394001-395000/394260.jpg) |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Connect the other end of the cable to your intended device.                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 3 | Slide the Power/Bluetooth slider up to turn on the headset.                                                                                                                                                                                             |
