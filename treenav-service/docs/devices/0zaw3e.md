# Turn your Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980 on and off

The B&O Cisco 980Power button is located on the bottom of the right ear cup.

| 1 | Press the Power button once to turn on your headset. |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Press the Power button again to turn it off.         |
