# Webex App | Audio and video calls

## Audio Calls

If you're calling someone from your desktop or mobile app, you can turn off the video before you start the call by making an [audio call](https://help.webex.com/article/ndl6910).

## Video Calls

Or, if you want the person to be able to see you, make your call a [video call](https://help.webex.com/article/4eliocb). You can do that from desktop, mobile, or web. If you change your mind, you can always [turn off your video](https://help.webex.com/article/xtc8md) during your call.

And if you'd prefer not to share your video in any of the calls that you receive, you can [turn off your video for all incoming calls](https://help.webex.com/article/6ewaiv) too. 

If you're [set up with Webex Calling](https://help.webex.com/article/nbtgrzx/), there are some things to note:

- When you make an audio call and then choose to change to a video call, video is only transmitted when the recipient turns on their video.
- When you make a video call and then either party decides to turn off their video, no video is transmitted for either side.
