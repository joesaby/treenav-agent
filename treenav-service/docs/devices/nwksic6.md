# Webex App | Leave a call when connected to a Webex device

| Click on ![](../../../../../../../en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/419001-420000/419181.JPG), and choose from the following: My Computer + **_device name_** —The call is ended on your computer and on the Webex device. My Computer—The call is only ended on your computer and continues on the Webex device. |
