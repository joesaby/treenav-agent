# Webex App | Join a meeting from search

| 1 | In the app header, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/417001-418000/417390.jpg) Search, meet, and call. Then, enter any of the following from an email invitation, or meeting notification: Meeting link Meeting number Video address |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Click Join in the search results, to join your meeting. If you enter incorrect meeting details or the details of a meeting you don't have permission to join, then you'll see a join failure notification.                                                                                              |
