# Activate or deactivate a poll in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

To activate a quiz, see [Activate or deactivate a quiz in Slido](https://help.webex.com/1bf2c3/).

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Apps \> Slido or click ⋯ \> Slido (polling and Q&A) in Webex Suite meeting platform.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| - | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select the Slido that contains the poll. If you select the wrong Slido, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459183.jpg) \> Recent Slidos.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 3 | In My interactions click Launch ![Activate poll](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459246.jpg) beside the poll to activate it. If you activated a survey, click ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459184.jpg) to show the first question to participants. As participants send their answers, you'll see the poll results update. |
| 4 | (Optional) Click Close voting ![Close voting](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459389.jpg) when you want to lock the voting. Participants won't be able to vote or change their mind even though the poll is still activated.                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 5 | Click Deactivate poll ![Deactivate poll](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/459001-460000/459247.jpg) when you want to end the poll and stop showing it to participants.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
