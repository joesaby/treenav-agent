# Webex App | Call anyone with a phone number

Access to a dial pad is available if you're set up with a phone service such as Unified CM, Webex Calling, or Calling hosted by a Service Provider. 

- [Desktop](#id%5F125261)
- [iOS](#id%5F125262)
- [Android](#id%5F129025)

| 2 | Go to Calling![Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/417001-418000/417356.jpg), enter a phone number in the dial pad, and then choose Audio call ![Audio Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/429001-430000/429940.jpg) or Video call ![Video Call button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438668.jpg). During your call, click More options ![More Options button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/450001-460000/450001-451000/450687.jpg) to access advanced calling features. Put your call on [hold](https://help.webex.com/article/npvltok) or [transfer](https://help.webex.com/article/5er4f3) to someone else. With some configurations, you can also [merge](https://help.webex.com/article/v4h0h4) calls or start a [conference call](https://help.webex.com/article/nx5rj7s). If your administrator has enabled call security for your organization, you'll see ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/442001-443000/442685.jpg) at the top right of your calling window, letting you know that the call is secure. Here are a few more ways to make calls to phone numbers: [Call from search](https://help.webex.com/article/iecbvc/)Highlight a number and use the [keyboard shortcut](https://help.webex.com/article/7wr87q/) to make a call.Enter alphanumeric phonewords, such as **1-800-FLOWERS** or **1-800-CONTACTS**, and Webex automatically converts the letters into numbers. |
