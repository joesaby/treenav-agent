# Turn on the Q&A feature in Slido

Slido is available in Webex Meetings and Webex Webinars. You can use Slido in Webex Meetings on [version 41.6 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/), and in Webex Webinars on [version 41.9 and later sites](https://help.webex.com/qgij81/) and in Webex Suite meeting platform. Slido polls and Q&A in webinars are available for up to 10,000 attendees, while quizzes are available for up to 5,000 participants.

By default, Q&A is turned off.

| 1 | During a meeting or webinar, go to Apps \> Slido.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Select an existing Slido or create a new one.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| 3 | Click the Q&A tab, and then click Enable Q&A to make the Q&A session live. [Change the Q&A settings](https://community.sli.do/setting-up-a-slido-event-82/event-settings-overview-844#Features+Settings) at <https://www.slido.com>, if you want to customize your Q&A session. Click Log In \> Log in with Webex, and enter your Webex username and password. |

If you don’t want to use the Q&A feature any longer, click  \> Settings \> Audience Q&A and toggle off the feature.

If you don't want participants to send any more questions but you still want to discuss and display the current questions, [close the Q&A session](https://help.webex.com/pummdi/) instead of turning it off.
