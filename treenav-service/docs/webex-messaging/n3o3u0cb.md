# Webex App | Turn Off @all Mentions

| In the space, go to Space Information ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427579.jpg), and then click Disable @mentions to all. ![disable @mentions to all](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/500001-600000/500001-510000/502001-503000/502657.jpg) You can only turn it off on desktop, but it's turned off for everyone using Webex App on mobile too. To enable @mentions to all again, you can go to Space Information ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/427001-428000/427579.jpg) and click Enable @mentions to all. |
