# Webex App | Clean up your voicemail trash

Messages remain in your Trash folder unless you:

- Recover a message. Then, the message moves back to your Inbox.
- Empty your trash.
- Leave your messages in your trash for a certain length of time and they're automatically removed. Your administrator determines how long messages stay in your trash.
