# Use Webex Audio in Webex Training

## Connect to Webex Audio

Webex Training provides the flexibility to connect audio in multiple ways when audio conferencing is turned on. For more information, see [Tips and tricks for using audio with Webex](https://collaborationhelp.cisco.com/article/0izy25).

| 1 | After you join the training session, select one of the following: Call Me–Enter a phone number, then select Call Me. You'll receive a call and may be prompted to press 1 to connect. I Will Call In–To choose this option, select the Use Phone drop-down list. Dial the phone number shown on your screen. When prompted, use your phone keypad to enter the access code and the Attendee ID shown on your screen. Your attendee ID connects your name in the meeting to your audio. If you don’t enter your attendee ID, your audio connection appears as a call-in user in the Participants list, separate from your name, and you can’t control audio from the application. If you don't have your attendee ID, press # to wait in the lobby until someone in the meeting lets you in. Call Using Computer–Choose this option to connect to audio using VoIP. To adjust your speaker and microphone settings, select Audio \> Speaker/Microphone Audio Test in the menu bar. ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/418001-419000/418254.jpg) |

[Mute or Unmute Others](#task%5F35072F0DE0E448BE0A1F2514E5647634)

If you are the training session host, you can mute any of the participants in your session. When you mute participants, only you can unmute them. They cannot unmute themselves.

| 1 | To mute or unmute specific people, go to the Participants panel, find their name, and select Mute ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/410001-420000/419001-420000/419396.jpg) or Unmute ![](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/420001-430000/420001-421000/420513.jpg). |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | You can mute everyone at once or you can mute people as they join. To mute everyone at once, go to the Participant menu and select Mute All or Unmute All.To mute everyone as they join, go to the Participant menu and select Mute on Entry.                                                                                                                  |
