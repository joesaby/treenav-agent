# Delete a virtual agent from Webex Contact Center

To delete a virtual agent: 

| 1 | Log in to the customer organization at <https://admin.webex.com>.                                                                  |
| - | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | From the navigation pane on the left, in the Servicessection, click Contact Center \> Tenant Settings \> Integrations \> Features. |
| 3 | Click the Virtual Agents  tab to view all Virtual Agent cards for your organization.                                               |
| 4 | Click the Delete  icon on the Virtual Agent card that you want to delete.                                                          |
| 5 | Click Delete  to confirm.                                                                                                          |
