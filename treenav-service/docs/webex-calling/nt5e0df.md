You can also [join a test meeting](https://help.webex.com/nti2f6w/) to check that you're ready.

What to do next

If you're having audio issues, check the following:

- Your computer’s volume is turned up.
- Your browser tab isn’t muted.
- Speakers or headphones are properly connected.
- Speakers or headphones are powered on.
- The volume is turned up on your output device.
