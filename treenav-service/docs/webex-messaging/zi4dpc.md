# Webex App | Start a conversation

- [iPhone and iPad](#task%5F3225F5B20C5CA8DCE75151DFE10F8C30)

| 1 | There are a few ways to start a conversation in Webex App: Go to Messaging ![Messaging button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438646.jpg) in the [navigation sidebar](https://help.webex.com/nx3i8h5/), and click ![Add button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/444001-445000/444067.jpg) \>  Send a message. Go to the app header, and click ![Add button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/444001-445000/444067.jpg) \>  Send a message. On web, to the right of the search bar, click ![Add button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/444001-445000/444067.jpg) \> Send a direct message. In a direct message space, at the top of the space, click Create a new message with <person> and others ![start a chat with others](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/470001-480000/477001-478000/477673.svg). |

| 1 | Go to Messaging ![Messaging button](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/430001-440000/438001-439000/438646.jpg), tap ![Start a message icon](https://cisco-api.ingeniuxondemand.com/DITA/content/en/us/td/i/400001-500000/440001-450000/448001-449000/448224.jpg), and select Send a direct message. |
| - | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 | Enter the person's name or email address or select them from your recents list. Then, just type your message and tap Send. When you type a greeting, such as—Hi, Hey, Hello, Good morning, Good afternoon, or Good evening, in a direct message space, the app automatically inserts the other persons first name.                            |
