# Schedule a Webex meeting on behalf of another host

To schedule a meeting on behalf of someone else, that person must have [allowed another host to schedule meetings on their behalf](https://help.webex.com/article/nub7z8c).

When you schedule a meeting for another host, both you and the other host can modify the meeting. You become a cohost of the meeting, but don't appear in the invitee list.

| 1 | Sign in to [User Hub](https://globalpage-prod.webex.com/signin), then click the Schedule drop-down menu and select Schedule a meeting. If you have any saved [meeting templates](https://help.webex.com/en-us/n19ao05/Use-a-Meeting-Template-in-Cisco-Webex-Meetings-Modern-View), you can select one from the Meeting templates drop-down menu. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 | In the Schedule for drop-down list, select the host's name for whom you're scheduling the meeting. To see this option, the person for whom you're scheduling the meeting must have [allowed another host to schedule meetings on their behalf](https://help.webex.com/article/nub7z8c).                                                          |
| 3 | Enter the details for the meeting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 4 | Select Schedule to schedule your meeting for later.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
