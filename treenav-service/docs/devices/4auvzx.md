When you use the Electronic Hookswitch (EHS):

- The EHS connects to the Cisco IP Phone 8811, 8841, and 8845 with the auxiliary port.
- The EHS connects to the Cisco IP Phone 8851, 8861, and 8865 with the auxiliary port, the USB port, or with Bluetooth.

You connect your headset to the phone with the Y-cable (RJ-9 connector and AUX connector). Your administrator may need to enable the headset so that you can use it.

## Cisco IP Conference Phone 7832 Multiplatform Phone Supported Accessories

The Cisco IP Conference Phone 7832 Multiplatform Phone does not support any accessories.

## Cisco IP Conference Phone 8832 Multiplatform Phone Supported Accessories

You can use the Cisco IP Conference Phone 8832 wired microphones and wireless microphones. You can't use wired and wireless microphones at the same time. 

## View the Accessories Associated with Your Phone

You can use the headset jack, Bluetooth, or the USB port to connect external hardware to your phone. The accessory list, by default, contains an analog headset that can be set up to enable wideband

Here are the Cisco headsets we support. For tips on setting up and using these headsets, see the following information for your model:

- [Cisco Headset 320 Series](https://help.webex.com//ldr8f8/)

- [Cisco Headset 530 Series](https://help.webex.com/32bbhq/)

- [Cisco Headset 720 Series](https://help.webex.com/ny0u85i/)

- [Bang & Olufsen Cisco 950](https://help.webex.com/ngc65i0/)
- [Bang & Olufsen Cisco 980](https://help.webex.com/tu9wyh/)

See the [Cisco Headset Compatibility Guide](https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/td/docs/voice%5Fip%5Fcomm/cuipph/Headsets/Compatibility%5FGuide/n500%5Fb%5Fheadset-compatibility.html) for detailed information on Cisco headset integration with your device.

You can also use third-party headsets. Check with the headset manufacturer to confirm whether you can use it with your Cisco phone.
